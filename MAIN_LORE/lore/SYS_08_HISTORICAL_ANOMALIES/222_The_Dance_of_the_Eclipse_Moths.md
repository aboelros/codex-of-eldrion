 I. The Luminal Migration: The Aetheric Insects of the 4th Dimension
The **Dance of the Eclipse Moths** is the primary historical anomaly documenting the catastrophic failure of the 4th Dimension’s biological light-cycle. To understand the event, one must first identify the role of the **Eclipse Moths**—a specialized species of Aetheric-insects that physically inhabit the light-gradients of the 4th Dimension. Unlike standard insects, the Moths do not consume organic matter; they consume **"Radiant-Frequency"** mana emitted by the dimension’s equatorial suns.

The Moths are the "Data-Storage-Unit" of the 4th Dimension’s ecosystem. Their wings are composed of **"Radiant-Silk"**—a high-purity biological-Aetheric material that physically "Records" the luminal history of the dimension. By the 3200th year of the Dark Age, the Moths’ migratory patterns had become the primary method for the **Photonic Cult** (see: 207) to transmit clandestine data packages between their mirror-cities. 

The Moths follow the **"Luminal-Anchor-Points"** of the dimension’s light-grid. However, the manifestation of the **False Sun** (see: 207) created a permanent **"Photonic-Interference"** anomaly that physically "Shattered" the Moths’ internal navigation.

 II. The "Photonic-Interference" Anomaly: Technical Specifications of the Death-Spiral
The anomaly was achieved through the creation of a secondary, non-local star that generated a terminal **"Radiant-Friction."**

1. **The Navigational-Override**: The Eclipse Moths utilize a specialized **"Luminal-Compass"**—a neural-array in their antennae that locks onto the highest-intensity light source. The False Sun, being 400% brighter than the natural sun, physically "Captured" the Moths' compasses.
2. **The Synchronized-Death-Spiral**: Instead of migrating across the dimension, the entire billion-strong swarm was pulled toward the heart of the False Sun. The Moths entered a terminal **"Feedback-Loop,"** circling the anomaly in a multi-layered, kaleidoscopic vortex of iridescent-blue and white fire. 
3. **The Harmonic-Dissolution**: As the Moths reached the False Sun’s event-horizon, their Radiant-Silk wings physically **"Ignited."** This created a secondary photonic surge—a global **"Light-Pulse"** that physically "Whited-Out" the 4th Dimension’s atmosphere for twelve hours.

Technical Specifications: "The Moths are currently a 'Luminal-Vortex.' They are no longer a species; they are a 'Friction-Engine.' The billion-fold vaporization of Radiant-Silk is generating enough 'Data-Debris' to physically blind the Panopticon in Sector 4-B for a full century. This is not a tragedy; it is the ultimate 'Signal-Jamming' event."

 III. The Tactical Log of the Solar-Flare: The Harvest of the Burning Wings
During the climax of the Dance, specialized Photonic Cult recovery-teams launched a high-stakes **"Harvest-Mission"** to recover the Radiant-Silk data-disks before they were annihilated.

The Mission Profile: **"Operation Silk-Grip"**
1. **The Deployment into the Vortex (Hour 0)**: Three cultist **"Light-Eaters"** (technical salvage units) ascended into the heart of the vortex using **"Mirror-Obsidian-Ships"** (see: 213). The ambient temperature was 2,000 degrees, but the ships were insulated with **"Void-Silt"** (see: 216).
2. **The Capture of the Data-Silk**: The salvage teams utilized **"Aetheric-Nets"** to physically "Fish" the burning moths from the vortex. They managed to recover twelve **"Silk-Cores"**—each containing the complete historical record of the 4th Dimension’s pre-war era.
3. **The Result**: The teams managed to extract the data before the False Sun’s final **"Radiant-Pulse"** whited-out the sector. This data became the foundation for the **Underground Railroad’s** 4th Dimension bypass-route. However, the Eclipse Moth species was officially reclassified as **"Ecologically-Extinct"** following the event.

Tactical Log Cultist Elara (no relation): "The vortex was beautiful. A billion wings of silver-glass, all burning as one. We got the cores. We have the memory of the Titans. But the sky... it's quiet now. The song of the moths is gone. Gabriel has siphoned the very color out of the air."

the scale of the tragedy manifest in the **Luminal-Plaza of the Mirror-City**, where a young resistance scribe named Kaelen (not the Warden) was witnessing the final hours of the Dance.
