2. **The 2nd Holy War (The Abyssal Incursion)**: The leaking power of the sealed Titans fused with the entropic chaos of the Abyss, giving birth to the **12 Demon Lords** (see: 223). The invasion of the upper realms necessitated the creation of the **"Ashborn Battery"** (see: 90), where the Gods used the Silent King Ashborn to slam the gates shut.
