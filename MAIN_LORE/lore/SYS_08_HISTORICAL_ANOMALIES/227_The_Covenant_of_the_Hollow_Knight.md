 I. The Covenant

In Year 1,450, a warrior operating in the resistance network of the 5th Dimension executed what the Vocation of Purity's medical review board subsequently classified as the most extreme Tier 2 Covenant ever documented in the Dark Age era.

The warrior the archive records the name Draven, which may be a pseudonym had been operating as a front-line defensive practitioner in the 5th Dimension's resistance networks for approximately thirty years. The specific operational context was a sustained Seraphic assault on a network refuge complex that had been ongoing for eleven days with no indication of Seraphic withdrawal. The refuge housed approximately 2,000 non-combatants. Draven was the senior surviving combat practitioner; his entire unit had been killed in the preceding eleven days.

Draven entered a Tier 2 Covenant with the following specific exchange: the surrender of all biological organs digestive system, respiratory system, cardiovascular system, thermal regulatory system, reproductive system, every organ not directly required for motor function and basic neural operation in exchange for the permanent, non-degrading structural integrity of his physical form at the biological moment of the Covenant's execution.

In practical terms: Draven's body became impenetrable. No Seraphic kinetic, thermal, or Acoustic strike caused any measurable physical damage.

In practical terms also: Draven became completely hollow. His chest and abdominal cavity were empty. His lungs did not function. His heart did not beat. He required no food and no water and no air.

---

 II. The Combat Performance

Draven's performance in the eleven subsequent hours following the Covenant is documented in detail by the two practitioners who survived the assault in the refuge complex.

He held the entrance corridor alone for those eleven hours while the evacuation of the 2,000 non-combatants was completed through the refugee complex's rear access routes. Every Seraphic strike impacted him and did nothing. When Seraphim attempted to grab or restrain him physically, their grip found no purchase the Covenant's structural integrity extended to his resistance to external physical manipulation.

He did not fight aggressively. He could not generate Aetheric output because his biology no longer supported Core processing the Core's maintenance systems required the biological functions he had surrendered. He simply stood in the corridor and could not be moved or damaged.

When the evacuation was complete, he walked out of the complex.

---

 III. The Madness

The phantom pain of the missing organs began immediately. The Covenant's exchange was precisely defined: the biological functions were gone. The nervous system's expectation of those functions was not.

Draven's neural architecture continued generating the sensory signals associated with every surrendered biological function as though those functions were present and attempting to operate. His body attempted to breathe with lungs that no longer existed. It attempted to circulate blood with a heart that was gone. It attempted to digest with an empty cavity.

The aggregate sensory experience of an entire body's biological function attempting to execute through a hollow vessel is not documented anywhere in the archive's medical literature because no equivalent case exists. Draven's own communications to the resistance network in the years following the Covenant describe it sparingly and with the specific vocabulary of someone attempting to describe something for which the language does not exist.

He remains active. The archive's last confirmed contact was approximately one hundred years ago. He is, as far as can be determined, still walking somewhere in the 5th Dimension, still impenetrable, still in continuous phantom pain, still alive without any biological definition of alive that the medical community can apply.

---

 Archivist Note: The Hollow
The Vocation of Purity's case review of the Draven Covenant resulted in a formal addition to the Covenant counseling protocols: a mandatory extended consultation for any practitioner presenting with a proposed exchange that involves surrender of biological function. The consultation's purpose is not to prevent such Covenants the Covenant architecture will execute them regardless of academic advice but to ensure the practitioner understands precisely what they are exchanging. Draven understood. He did it anyway because 2,000 people needed eleven hours and he had the specific currency required. The archive records this without offering a cleaner resolution than the one that exists: he is alive, and they escaped, and he is still paying.
