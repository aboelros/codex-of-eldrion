 I. The Physics of the Failure: The Structural Decay of the Gravitational Constant
The **Weeping of Lithos** is the primary historical anomaly documenting the catastrophic failure of the 2nd Dimension’s localized physics. To understand the event, one must first identify the role of **Titan Lithos** (see: 153). Lithos was the architect of universal mass and the gravitational constants governing the 13 Dimensions. During the **Grand Treason** (see: 151), Lithos was forced into **Thermodynamic-Stasis** (see: 205), leaving his gravitational maintenance subroutines to run on autopilot.

By the 312th year of the Dark Age, the foundational architecture of the 2nd Dimension had undergone significant **"Calibration-Drift."** Without the active monitoring of its designer, the gravitational processing stack began to accumulate microscopic errors. In Oakhaven—the greatest surviving industrial city of the Iron Born—these errors reached a terminal threshold.

The event was not caused by a military strike or a ritual. It was a **"Numerical-Overflow"** in the universe’s operating system. The localized gravitational constant did not just fluctuate; it **"Inverted."**

 II. The Sixty Seconds: Tactical Log of the Inversion Cycle
The destruction of Oakhaven occurred in exactly sixty seconds, during the summer solstice of Year 312.

1. **Second 0-10 (The Inversion ignited)**: At 11:00 hours, the gravitational constant governing a 40-square-kilometer zone surrounding Oakhaven flipped to its simultaneous inverse. Mass within the zone did not attract; it repelled. Structures were physically "Disconnected" from the ground as the earth pushed them upward.
2. **Second 15-40 (The Convergence-Pivot)**: As the initial repulsion reached its maximum, the system’s **"Auto-Correction"** subroutine triggered. In a desperate attempt to re-stabilize the zone, the subroutines generated a **"Convergent-Pressure-Wave."** Every building, every machine, and every one of the 400,000 residents was pulled inward toward the city's center plaza at the inverted gravitational maximum.
3. **Second 45-60 (The Compression-Finale)**: The pressure reached **"Tier-5 Density."** The basalt structures of the city, the iron-foundries, and the biological mass of the people were compressed together in a micro-second. The resulting force was so immense that it physically "Annealed" the city into a single, hyper-dense object.

Technical Specifications: "The Oakhaven object is a 'Singularity-in-Stasis.' It is a sphere three meters in diameter, composed of perfectly compressed, Relic-grade material equivalent to **Aetheric-Diamond.** It possesses the mass of an entire dimension's industrial capital, but the volume of a large boulder."

 III. The Regulatory Consequence: Gabriel’s Strategic Opportunism
Following the event, Gabriel utilized the **"Weeping"** as the primary justification for the total ban on unauthorized Gravitational Aether research across all dimensions.

1. **The Tier-3 Moratorium**: The Inquisition declared that any mortal experiment involving mass-manipulation could trigger a similar, or larger, global inversion. This effectively killed the Iron Born’s technological edge, as they could no longer refine the high-density materials required for **Relic-Forging.**

In reality, the Panopticon is simply performing **"Low-Level-Triage"** on the Titans' failing subroutines. 3. **The Weaponization of Fear**: By keeping the Oakhaven object in its original crater, Gabriel created a permanent **"Monument to Mortal Failure."** It serves as a psychological deterrent, a humming reminder that unauthorized magic leads to the physical deletion of everything you love.

Technical Log Warden Kael: "The Oakhaven object is the most efficient PR tool in Gabriel's arsenal. He doesn't need to execute researchers if he can prove that their research will implode their own families. The 'Weeping' was a gift to the Inquisition."
