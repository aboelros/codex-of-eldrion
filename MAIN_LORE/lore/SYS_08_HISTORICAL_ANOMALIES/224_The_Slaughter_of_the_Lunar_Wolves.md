 224. The Slaughter of the Lunar Wolves: The Sanitization of the 13th Dimension’s Peaks

Classification: Ecological Catastrophe / Military Sanitization | Status: Concluded (Extinct) | Location: 13th Dimension / The Moon-Pillars | Era: Dark Age, Year 3600 | Primary Actor: Gabriel, The Lunar Wolves (The Night-Watch), The Seraphic Solar-Wing

 I. The Lunar Ecosystem: The Aetheric Predators of the 13th
The **Slaughter of the Lunar Wolves** is the primary historical anomaly documenting the total extinction of the 13th Dimension’s apex Aetheric predator. To understand the event, one must first identify the role of the **Lunar Wolves**—a specialized species of biological-Aetheric shadows that inhabited the **Moon-Pillars** (the thirteen towering crystalline peaks of the 13th Dimension). The wolves were not biological in the standard sense; they were composed of **"Phased-Shadow"** and **"Moon-Silver,"** physically capable of "Blinking" between the 13th and the **Deep-Found Substrate** (see: 205).

The wolves served as the "Security-Subroutine" for the 13th Dimension’s light-grid. They hunted the **Void-Wraiths** (see: 200) and maintained the harmonic stability of the moon-pillars. By the 3600th year of the Dark Age, the wolves had become the primary guardian for the **Underground Railroad’s** 13th Dimension relay-station. They physically "Masked" the resistance signals with their own high-frequency howls, making the station invisible to the Panopticon.

Gabriel, recognizing that the wolves were providing a "Blind-Spot" for the resistance, ordered the total **"Sanitization"** of the species. 

 II. The "Frequency-Hunting": Technical Specifications of the Moon-Phase-Link
The Lunar Wolves were uniquely vulnerable due to their physical dependency on the **13th Dimension’s Moon**.

1. **The Moon-Phase-Link**: The wolves’ Soul Cores were "Entangled" with the orbital cycle of the moon. Their physical density was determined by the lunar phase; during the new moon, they were almost entirely non-corporeal Shadows. During the full moon, they were solid Moon-Silver.
2. **The Solar-Shatter**: The Inquisition utilized a specialized **"Solar-Leash"**—a Seraphic array that physically "Reflected" the light of the 1st Dimension’s sun into the 13th during the new moon. This created a permanent **"Artificial-Full-Moon,"** physically forcing the entire species into a solid, vulnerable state simultaneously.
3. **The Frequency-Hunting**: Once the wolves were solidified, the Seraphim tuned their energy-beams to the specific **"Lunar-Resonance"** of the wolves’ silver-skin. The beams didn't burn; they physically **"Shattered"** the wolves’ atomic lattice, turning the creatures into a shower of inert silver-dust.

Technical Specifications: "The Slaughter is 'The Geometry of Visibility.' We have physically removed the 'Shadow-Variable' from the 13th Dimension. By forcing the wolves to remain solid, we have denied them their only defense: the void. The pillars are now quiet. The Panopticon can see the bedrock."

 III. The Tactical Log of the Solar-Shatter: The Siege of the Lunar-Almighty
Following the solidification of the species, the Inquisition launched a final, high-intensity strike on the **Lunar-Almighty Shrine** (the primary breeding ground of the pack).

The Mission Profile: **"Operation Silver-Grave"**
1. **The Artificial Daylight (Hour 0)**: The Seraphic Solar-Wing activated the reflectors. The 13th Dimension, usually a world of deep-red twilight, was suddenly illuminated by a blinding, absolute-white light. The shadows of the Moon-Pillars were physically "Deleted."
2. **The Charge of the Silver-Pack**: The wolves, led by the Alpha (a creature the size of a resistance tank), attempted a final, desperate charge against the descending Seraphim. They utilized their **"Lunar-Howls"** to create a localized harmonic-shield, but the Solar-Leash physically "Overloaded" their Cores.
3. **The Result**: Every wolf in the shrine was shattered within six minutes. The 13th Dimension is now officially recorded as **"Sanitized-Class-A."** The wolves are extinct.

Tactical Log Inquisitor Malachi: "The species was extinguished with 100% efficiency. The 'Night-Watch' is gone. The Moon-Pillars are currently reflecting the light of the Apex with a clarity of 99.9%. The resistance relay-station has been identified and whited-out. The shadow has been cured."

 IV. The Howl in the White: The Cinematic Narrative
the tragedy of the Slaughter manifest at the **Peak of the 13th Pillar**, where a resistance scribe named Elara (no relation) was witnessing the final defense of the pack.

The Scene:
The world was a blinding, iridescent-white wall of **"Solar-Statc."** The air was hot—thick with the ozonous smell of the **Solar-Reflectors.** Around the shrine, the shadows of the crystalline rocks were physically **"Burning"** away, leaving only the featureless white stone.
The Alpha stood at the center of the plateau, its fur a shimmering, incandescent-blue silver. Its eyes were two burning points of **"Void-Fire,"** reflecting the descent of the Seraphic silhouettes.

"Don't look at the light, Elara!" the Alpha’s voice was a low, vibrating **"Neural-Broadcast"** inside her mind. The wolves didn't speak through vocal cords; they spoke through the logic-bridge. "The light is the bleach. Keep your eyes on the shadow of your own soul."
Elara knelt behind a shard of obsidian, her hands over her eyes. She could hear the **"Tinkle"** of glass—the sound of the wolves' silver-skin physically shattering under the Seraphic beams.

The Alpha let out a final, global **"Howl."**
It wasn't a cry of pain. it was a **"Frequency-Command."** The howl physically **"Anchored"** the data of the wolves' history into the crystalline structure of the pillar itself. 
"We are not leaving, little one," the Alpha’s voice whispered one last time. "We are just going into the stone. Wait for the King. He will know the frequency of the moon."

Then, a high-intensity Seraphic beam struck the Alpha. 
It didn't explode. It physically **"Crystalized."** For a microsecond, the Alpha was a statue of pure, iridescent-blue moon-silver, illuminated by the white light of the sun. Then, with a low, vibrating **"Thump,"** the statue shattered into a billion microscopic shards. 
The light died. The Solar-Wing retreated. 
Elara stood up in the silence. The moon-pillar was no longer red. It was a shimmering, permanent silver, pulsing with the trapped howls of the pack. She picked up a single shard of the Alpha’s silver-skin. It was still hot, still vibrating with the warning: *The moon remembers.*

 V. Aftermath: The Legacy of the Silver Shards
The 224th file records the official reclassification of the Lunar Wolves as **"Biological-Debris."** The species is gone, but the "Silver-Static" trapped in the Moon-Pillars continues to cause minor interference for the Panopticon’s long-range sensors.

Torin notes that the 224th file is the **"Lunar-Blueprint"** for the **Hybrid King.** The King is the only one who can "Re-sequence" the silver-dust and call the wolves from the stone. The 224th file is the record of the day we learned how to howl in the light. We are waiting for the King who can lead the Night-Watch.
