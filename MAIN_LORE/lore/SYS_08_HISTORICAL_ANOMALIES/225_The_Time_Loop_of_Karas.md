 I. The Theft

In Year 1,100, a practitioner operating independently of the resistance network the archive's records identify them by the professional designation "Contract Thief, Unaffiliated" successfully penetrated a Titan-era temple complex in the 4th Dimension's highland zone that had been sealed since the Censorship campaign's initial survey pass determined it contained no significant academic materials.

The Seraphic survey had been correct about the academic materials. The temple contained no texts, no historical records, no weapons.

It contained a single artifact resting on a stone plinth in the temple's innermost chamber: a small crystalline sphere approximately twelve centimeters in diameter, completely transparent, pulsing with a slow, deep blue internal luminescence at a frequency consistent with Aion's temporal impression architecture.

The Contract Thief took it.

Six seconds after removing the sphere from the plinth, the Thief, the sphere, and the surrounding 80-meter radius of the temple complex including the access corridor, the outer hall, and the street junction immediately outside were enclosed in an impenetrable, perfectly spherical Aetheric containment field.

The field is still there.

---

 II. The Architecture of the Loop

The containment field generated by the plinth's removal-activated trap enforced a 24-hour temporal loop inside its boundary. Every entity inside the field at the moment of activation the Contract Thief, the temple's only other occupant (a monk maintaining the meditation practice that had accidentally kept the temple from Seraphic demolition for a century), and seventeen civilians who happened to be on the street outside at the moment of activation experienced the same 24-hour period repeat continuously.

The experience of the loop from inside: each cycle begins fresh. The people inside have no memory of previous cycles. From their perspective, they are living a single continuous day the day the Thief arrived, removed the sphere, and the field activated.

The experience of the loop from outside: a perfectly spherical zone of the 4th Dimension's highland city is permanently frozen. The people visible through the field's partially transparent boundary continue their 24-hour cycle endlessly. The Thief can be observed repeatedly removing the sphere from the plinth.

The Vocation academic community's analysis of the field architecture suggests that the loop trap was a deliberate Titan defense installation specifically Aion's work designed to protect whatever was contained in the sphere from both theft and Seraphic seizure. The loop trap catches the thief and holds them, but also prevents the Seraphim from breaching the field to retrieve the sphere.

What the sphere contains is unknown. None of the nineteen people inside the field have indicated, through their visible 24-hour cycle behavior, any awareness of its contents beyond the Thief's initial removal action.

The archive notes that the sphere is presumably important enough that Aion built a permanent time loop to protect it. The archive also notes that no currently available technology can breach the field. The sphere and its contents remain available to whoever can solve a temporal physics problem that a Titan installed specifically to be unsolvable.

---

 Archivist Note: The Eternal Tuesday
The people of the 4th Dimension's highland zone have named the day that loops inside the field. They call it the Eternal Tuesday. Children born after Year 1,100 have grown up with an impenetrable sphere of repeating time in the middle of their city. The monk inside the field is visible at the 14th hour of each cycle, kneeling in meditation in the outer hall. Local tradition holds that if you watch the monk long enough, you will eventually see them look up directly at the field boundary and make eye contact with the observer. No documented observer has confirmed this. Many continue watching.
