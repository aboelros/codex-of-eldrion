 I. The Ossuary of the 6th Dimension: The Relic of the Titanomachy
The **Resurrection of the Bone Dragon** is the primary historical anomaly documenting the catastrophic re-activation of a pre-war biological-Aetheric construct. To understand the event, one must first identify the role of the **Bone Dragon**—a massive, fossilized skeleton remaining from the **Titanomachy** (see: 151). Originally known as **Shade-Slayer**, the dragon was a biological-Aetheric weapon created by the **Titans** to purge siphoned-Aether "Blooms" (see: 196) during the early days of the conflict.

The dragon was not a living creature; it was a **"Necrotic-Regulator."** Its bones were composed of high-density **"Aetheric-Calcium,"** physically capable of absorbing the residual soul-noise of a dying dimension. For three thousand years, the skeleton remained buried in the mineral-fields of the 6th Dimension, a silent graveyard of the pre-monarch era. 

However, by the 3400th year of the Dark Age, the **Seraphic Dampeners** in the 6th Dimension underwent a catastrophic **"Soul-Grafting"** error. Instead of siphoning the mana into the Apex, the local array physically "Injected" the stolen souls of a nearby sanctuary into the fossilized bedrock.

The dragon was now a **"Mineral-Organic-Construct"** powered by the grief of a thousand stolen souls.
3. **The Harmonic-Scream**: The dragon’s re-activation emitted a high-frequency **"Death-Static"** that physically "De-coupled" the local Seraphic sensors. To the **Panopticon**, the 6th Dimension’s fossilized plains appeared as a massive, pulsing "Blind-Spot."

Technical Specifications: "The Bone Dragon is 'The Hunger of the Past.' It has woken up in a world where there is no longer enough Aether to sustain its regulatory subroutines.
