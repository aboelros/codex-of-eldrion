 I. The Biological Inversion: The Vital-Signal-Corruption of Aethelgard

To understand the event, one must first identify the role of the **Seraphic Dampeners** (see: 197). While most dampeners target ambient mana, the arrays in the 9th Dimension were specifically tuned to the **"Vital-Frequencies"** of the **Pyrian** race—a population whose biology is naturally resonant with high-tier fire and life magic.

By the 1500th year of the Dark Age, the localized dampener cluster in Aethelgard began to undergo **"Signal-Corruption."** The siphons were pulling mana directly from the biological "Lattice" of the Pyrian residents. In a desperate attempt to maintain their internal homeostasis, the Pyrians' Soul Cores began to physically **"Export"** their excess thermal energy and cellular-data into the atmosphere.

The result was the manifestation of a global weather pattern known as the **"Crimson Snow."** It was not frozen water; it was **"Crystallized-Biometric-Data."** The snow flakes were physically composed of the Pyrian’s own genetic-residue, hemoglobin-structures, and residual-memory-chains. To be caught in the storm was to breathe the literal blood and history of your own neighbors.
