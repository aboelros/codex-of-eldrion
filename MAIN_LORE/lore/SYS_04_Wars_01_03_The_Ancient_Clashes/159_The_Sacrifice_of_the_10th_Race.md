 159. The Sacrifice of the 10th Race

Classification: 1st Holy War / Biological Atrocity | Status: Supreme Canonical Record | Primary Actors: Juno (The Weaver), Malthus, The Human-Celestial Populace

---

 I. The 10th Race: Primordial Innocence and the Divine Mandate
Before the 1st Holy War, the mortal inhabitants of the 13 Dimensions—collectively known as the **10th Race** (comprising precursors to humans, high-celestials, and the original forest-dwellers)—occupied a position of "Protected-Stasis." Under the Titans, they were not soldiers or workers; they were the **"Biological-Reflections"** of the architects' laws. Their lives were long, their bodies were resistant to disease, and their minds were permanently synchronized to the "Harmonic-Sync" of the universe.

The ecology of the 10th Race was one of absolute pastoral perfection. Cities like the **Golden Vale** were built from living Aether-trees that responded to the music of the inhabitants. There was no concept of industry because the Titans provided all resources through the Ley-lines. However, this perfection was a "Conceptual-Leash." The 10th Race was physically incapable of independent innovation—they could only build what the Titans had already dreamed.

Zenithos recognized that this race was the Monarchs' greatest obstacle. As long as millions of mortals remained synchronized to the Titans, their collective "Will" acted as a massive stabilizing-buffer for the architects' laws. To kill the gods, the Monarchs first had to "Disconnect" the children. 

 II. The Protocol of the Living Heat-Sinks: Technical Specifications
The forging of the Nexus Chains and the God Blight (see: 153 and 158) required a level of entropic-energy that would physically destroy any divine material. Malthus discovered that the only thing capable of "Absorbing" the heat of the Abyssal virus without immediately evaporating was a **Sentient Soul Core.** 

The **"Heat-Sink Protocol"** was the systematic weaponization of 10th Race lives. The Monarchs realized that a mortal soul possessed a "Low-Frequency-Stability" that acted as a perfect insulator. By channeling Abyssal entropy through a living mortal, the Monarchs could "Filter" the chaos, making it stable enough to be forged into weapons.

1. **The Soul-Grafting**: Mortal "Volunteers" (harvested from loyalist cities) were placed in **"Resonance-Barrels."** Their Aetheric-signatures were physically "Un-stitched" until they became blank biological canvases. 
2. **The Entropic-Filtering**: Raw Abyssal-Draft was pumped through the mortals' neural networks. The mortals acted as **"Biological-Resistors,"** burning up their own life-force to slow down the entropic-decay of the weapons being forged. 
3. **The Yield**: On average, one hundred 10th Race celestials were required to stabilize a single link of a Nexus Chain. The mortals did not die in the biological sense; their souls were physically "Smeared" into the metal, forever trapped as the fuel that keeps the Titans bound.

III. The Burning of the Vale: The Cinematic Narrative
The largest single sacrifice occurred during the **"Inversion of the Golden Vale,"** a loyalist human-celestial stronghold that refused to acknowledge Zenithos as the new Architect. 

The Scene:
The Golden Vale sat in the 5th Dimension, a paradise of white marble and singing water. The inhabitants—thousands of the original 10th Race—stood at the gates, their hands raised in the "Titan-Sync-Gesture," believing that the Architect Luminaria would protect them.
Zenithos did not arrive with a legion. He arrived with a single **"Entropy-Siphon."** Beside him stood Juno, her hands moving through the air as if she were untying a knot.
"They still believe in the songs, Zenithos," Juno whispered, her silver-steel eyes reflecting the golden sun of the Vale. "They are trying to sing the light back into existence."
"Then give them a new song," Zenithos replied.

Juno activated the Siphon. The sky over the Vale did not turn dark; it turned **"Transparent."** The inhabitants' connection to the Titan Luminaria was physically "Un-plugged." In a single, agonizing second, the silence of the Abyss flooded the Vale. 
The people did not scream. As their Soul Cores were siphoned into the device to power the next batch of God Blight, they simply **"Faded."** Color leaked from their skin, their eyes, and their clothes. They became grey, translucent statues of original-sin. 
The Golden Vale transformed from a paradise into the first **"Grey-Wasteland."** The water stopped flowing. The Aether-trees turned to anthracite-black charcoal. In less than ten minutes, ten thousand years of biological evolution were converted into three milligrams of stabilized Abyssal-entropy.

Zenithos watched the last celestial fade into grey silt. "This is the true cost of freedom," he spoke to the empty air. "We must burn the children to keep the fathers in the dark."

 IV. The First Genetic-Scars: The Legacy of the Sacrifice
 The 159th file records the moment the "Mortal-Condition" was born. Before the Sacrifice, the 10th Race did not know fear, grief, or aging.

1. **The Biological-Decay**: The "Siphoning" left a permanent trauma in the surviving mortal populations. The Ley-lines were no longer "Pure," leading to the mutation of the mortal genome. This was the birth of **"Entropy-Aging"**—the process where a mortal’s soul slowly un-ravels over 80-100 years.
2. **The "Great-Aphasia"**: Survivors found they could no longer "Hear" the music of the Titans. This created a profound psychological vacuum that the Monarchs filled with the **"Cult of Authority."**
3. **The Hybrid Potential**: Archivist Torin notes that the Sacrifice was the unintentional planting of the **"Hybrid-Seed."** By saturating the mortal population with Abyssal entropy, the Monarchs created the biological foundation for a being who could one day survive both the Gold and the Grave. The **Hybrid King** is the ultimate descendant of those who survived the siphoning—the one who remembers the heat of the burning Vale.

 V. Aftermath: The Silence of the Vale
The 159th file is the darkest record in the archive. It proves that the Monarchs' rule is not built on "Better Laws," but on "Better Recycling." We are the recycled products of a war we were never meant to fight.

Archivist Torin’s Synthesis: We live in a universe of shadows because we are the ancestors of the children who were used as batteries. The "Cold" we feel in our hearts during the winter is the phantom echo of the Golden Vale siphoning. We are waiting for the **Hybrid King** to give us back the fire they stole. For only he who has the blood of the battery and the fire of the architect can truly light a sun that never goes out.
