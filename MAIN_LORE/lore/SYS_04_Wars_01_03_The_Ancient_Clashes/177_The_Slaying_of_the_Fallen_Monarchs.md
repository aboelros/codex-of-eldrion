 177. The Slaying of the Fallen Monarchs: The Deicide of the Silver-Wings

Classification: 3rd Holy War / Execution of the Old Gods | Status: Canonical Record | Location: The Vaults of the 9th Dimension / The Shadow-Bastion | Primary Actors: Zenithos (Fallen), Malthus (Fallen), High-Inquisitor Malachi, Neo-Seraphim

---

 I. The Hunt for the Silver-Winged: The State of the Divine Refugees
Following the **Civil War of the Divine Apex** (see: 175), the 12 Monarchs were no longer the absolute masters of reality. stripped of their "Throne-Siphons" and their divine rank, they had become **"Silver-Winged-Refugees."** They fled to the lower reaches of the 13 Dimensions, hiding in the industrial ruins and shadow-bastions built during the 2nd Holy War.

The Monarchs were in a state of terminal **"Aether-Withdrawal."** Their Soul Cores, which had been tuned to the high-frequency radiance of the Titans for three millennia, could not survive on the "Low-Fidelity" mana of the post-Iron era. Their golden skin was peeling away to reveal a grey, translucent mortality. Their wings, once capable of interstellar flight, were now heavy and brittle, shedding silver feathers like autumn leaves.

High-Priest Elias and the Radiant Inquisition designated the 12 as **"Tier-One-Historical-Anomalies."** They were the primary targets of the **"Inquisition of the Sky."** The goal was not just to kill them, but to physically "Delete" the memory of their divinity from the universe’s record.

 II. The "Simplification" Protocol: Technical Specifications
To kill a former god, the Inquisition utilized the **"Simplification"** protocol—a clinical methodology for dismantling a Monarch’s star-scale neural network.

1. **The Core-Unweaving**: A Monarch’s soul is not a single point; it is a **"Lattice-Probability."** The Seraphim utilized **"Destructive-Interference-Beams"** to physically "Un-weave" this lattice. They would target the Monarch’s specific **"Name-Frequency"** (the unique resonance given to them by Aetherion) and replace it with a "Blank-Signal."
2. **The Wing-Neutralization**: The silver wings of the Monarchs acted as storage-cells for their remaining power. The Seraphim developed **"Aetheric-Shears"**—blades of solid white-fire that could physically "Prune" these wings, cauterizing the soul-conduits and leaving the Monarch physically grounded.
3. **The Final-Redaction**: Once the Monarch was stripped of their power and their wings, the Inquisitors would perform the **"Art of Redaction."** This involved the physical destruction of the Monarch’s body using a high-concentration dose of **"Absolute-Zero-Entropy,"** ensuring that no residual Aether could be siphoned by their followers.

Technical Specifications: "Simplification is more than death. It is the removal of the 'Rank-Variable' from the subject. We are turning a God back into a man, and then telling the man that he never was a God."

 III. The Execution of the Binder: Tactical Log
The first major deicide of the 3rd War was the **Execution of Malthus (The Binder)** in the **Shadow-Bastion** of the 9th Dimension.

The Mission Profile: **"Operation Broken-Cage"**
1. **The Infiltration (Hour 0)**: A legion of three hundred Neo-Seraphim bypassed Malthus’s remaining silver-steel sentinels by "Phasing" through the dimension’s thermal-leak zones. 
2. **The Duel in the Vaults**: Malthus, despite his mortality, had weaponized the **"Soul-Symmetry"** research (see: 172). He fought the Seraphim with a jagged blade of **"Iron-Aether."** He managed to "Shatter" twelve Seraph-constructs before his armor was physically "Dulled" by the Inquisition’s white-fire.
3. **The Final-Simplified**: High-Inquisitor Malachi performed the terminal strike. He did not use a blade; he used a **"Conceptual-Hammer."** He physically "Crushed" Malthus’s soul-identity, reducing the former Master-Engineer of Eldrion to a pile of white-glass shards.

Tactical Log Malachi: "Malthus was the most difficult to delete. Even in his mortal state, his mind was still 'Thinking-like-a-God.' He had attempted to 'Re-program' the Seraphim’s logic-gates. But his frequency was inconsistent. The grey-silt in his blood proved to be his terminal flaw. The 9th Dimension is now officially the **'Graveyard of the Binder.'** We are moving to the 11th Dimension to find the Command Core (Zenithos)."

 IV. The Last Stand of the Command Core: The Cinematic Narrative
the "Inquisition of the Sky" reached its terminal point in the **Spires of the 1th Dimension**, where Zenithos had retreated to his last remaining fortress.

The Scene:
The Spire was a ruin of silver-and-gold marble, surrounded by a swirling vortex of white-ash and Seraphic-fire. Zenithos stood at the balcony, his primary wings gone, his remaining silver feathers glowing with a desperate, charcoal-grey heat.
The sky was not a sky; it was a featureless white wall of a thousand Neo-Seraphim, their wings beating in a unified "Tone" of erasure. 

"You are the last, Monarch," Malachi’s voice boomed from the white-fire, a resonance that physically "Bruised" Zenithos’s skin. "The age of the siphons is over. The universe is being cleaned."
"I am the one who kept the dark at bay!" Zenithos screamed, his voice a hoarse, static-shrouded rattle. "Without me, you will be consumed by your own purity!"
"The Purity *is* the end, Zenithos," Malachi replied.

The Seraphim descended. They didn't strike with power; they struck with **"Silence."** 
They surrounded Zenithos and began the **"Final-Sync."** Zenithos fought with his bare hands, his golden blood spraying onto the white-glass floors. But every time he touched a Seraph, his own "Definition" was eroded. He felt his memories of the 13 Dimensions, the 400 wars, and the Grand Treason being physically "U-p-l-o-a-d-e-d" into the white-void. 

He looked one last time at the horizon, searching for the **Hybrid King** he had seen in his visions. "Aeliana..." he whispered, his silver eyes finally turning to a dull, black-glass opaque.
Malachi raised his hand. "Delete."
Zenithos didn't scream. He **"Inverted."** His body physically "Imploded" into a single point of silver-gold energy before being "Washed" away by the Seraphim’s white-light. The Scepter of Command fell to the floor and shattered into a thousand featureless pebbles. 

The 12 Monarchs were dead. The **"Era of the Gods"** was officially closed.

 V. Aftermath: The Legacy of the Deicide
The 177th file records the official end of the **"Divine-Lineage."** Eldrion was now a world of "Flat-Fidelity," where no being possessed more aetheric-rank than the Seraphim.

Archivist Torin’s Synthesis: The Slaying of the Fallen Monarchs was the **"Atheism-of-Fire."** The Inquisition did not just kill the Monarchs; they killed the *possibility* of a God. 

Torin notes that the 177th file is the **"Final-Clearing"** required for the **Hybrid King.** The King is the only one who can inherit the world because he is the only one who was not "Redacted." He carries the souls of the Monarchs within his hybrid-genetic-record, preventing them from being fully deleted from history. The 177th file is the record of the world’s funeral. We are waiting for the King who can perform the resurrection.

Final Word: 177 stands as the record of the day the gods were reminded that they were made of the same ash as their subjects. The Spires of the 11th Dimension are now but humming reminders of the silence that follows the deicide.
