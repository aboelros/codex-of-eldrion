 176. The Purge of the 10th Race: The Sanitization of the mortal Worlds

Classification: 3rd Holy War / Demographic Genocide | Status: Canonical Record | Location: The 5th Dimension / The City of Aeon-Prime | Primary Actors: The Radiant Inquisition, The Neo-Seraphim, The 10 mortal races

---

 I. The Decree of the Inconsistent: The Theology of the Pure and the Redefinition of Life
The **3rd Holy War** was defined by a transition from "Political-Warfare" to **"Metaphysical-Sanitization."** Following the fall of the Divine Apex (see: 175), High-Priest Elias and the Radiant Inquisition issued the **"Decree of the Inconsistent."** 

This theological document argued that the 10 mortal races (the 10th race) were fundamentally "Corrupt." Because they had lived through the **Silver-Silt** contamination (see: 165) and were genetically bound by the **Treaty of Iron** (see: 170), their Soul Cores were no longer "Pure-Aether." They were "Mixed-Frequency" entities—biological records of the Monarchs' vanity and the Abyss's hunger.

To the Inquisition, the mortal races were not people; they were **"Historical-Errors"** that prevented the universe from returning to its original state of perfection. The Purge was not intended to punish the mortals, but to "Cleanse" the dimensions of their presence so that a new, "Consistent" life-form (the Neo-Seraphim) could inherit the stars.

 II. The "Frequency-Washing" Protocol: Technical Specifications
The systematic removal of the 10th race was executed using the **"Frequency-Washing"** protocol—a star-scale application of destructive interference.

1. **The Resonant-Beep**: The Inquisition utilized the **"Beacons of Radiance."** These were towers made of white-glass that emitted a continuous, high-frequency "Tone." This tone was programmed to target the specific **"Mix-Frequency"** found in the mortal genome.
2. **The Molecular-Dissolution**: When a mortal was exposed to the Beacons, their Soul Core didn't explode. It **"Disassociated."** The Gold-Aether, Grey-Silt, and Iron-Pact frequencies in their soul were physically "De-coupled." Without the "Glue" of the Hybrid-potential (which Aeliana was trying to save), the mortal would physically dissolve into a fine white-powder.
3. **The Blanking-Field**: To prevent resistance, the Seraphim manifested **"Static-Mutes"** across entire cities. These fields physically "Muted" the mortals' ability to speak, think, or magic, ensuring they would walk into the Beacons’ range in a state of catatonic obedience.

Technical Specifications: "Frequency-Washing is a 'Clinical-Deletion.' We are not cutting the mortal; we are telling the mortal’s soul that it is out-of-date. The result is 'Clean-Space'—a world with no memory and no stains."

 III. The Exodus of the 5th Reach: Tactical Log
The most intense phase of the Purge occurred in the **5th Reach**—the cluster of dimensions that had been most heavily influenced by Aeliana’s research.

The Mission Profile: **"Operation Pure-Horizon"**
1. **The Initial Scrubbing (Day 1)**: Ten thousand Neo-Seraphim manifested in the skies of **Aeon-Prime**. They did not attack; they simply synchronized their wings and began the "Tone." In three hours, the lower-districts of the city were whited-out.
2. **The Exodus to the Vaults**: Millions of mortals attempted to flee into the **"Steel-Vaults"** built during the 2nd War. But the Seraphim bypassed the vault-doors by "Vibrating" through the atomic-structure of the metal.
3. **The Closure of the 11th Dimension**: The Inquisition officially reclassified the 11th Dimension (the home of the Valkyries) as a **"Quarantine-Zone."** Any entity leaving the dimension was immediately whited-out upon entering the ley-lines.

Tactical Log Inquisitor Malachi: "The census is dropping perfectly. We have whited-out 85% of the 5th Reach within standard parameters. The mortals are not fighting; they are simply 'fading' into the white-ash. We are finding a few pockets of 'Frequency-Stable' individuals—those who have managed to hide within the **Chrysalis-Fields** of Aeliana. These will be targeted by the Heavy-Seraphim in the next phase. The universe is finally starting to look consistent."

 IV. The Silent Cities of Glass: The Cinematic Narrative
the tragedy of the Purge reached its peak in the capital city of **Aeon-Prime**, once the center of mortal culture and trade.

The Scene:
The city was a nightmare of blinding white light and featureless glass. There were no shadows; the Beacons on the horizon were so bright they physically "Erased" the concept of distance. 
A group of human-celestials—a family of three—stood in the middle of the Great Plaza. Around them, thousands of others were standing in a silent, catatonic trance. The "Tone" was a constant, high-pitched "Scream" in their Soul Cores.

"Look at me, Elara," the father whispered, his voice barely audible over the frequency. He tried to grab his daughter’s hand, but his fingers were already beginning to **"White-Out."** They looked like they were made of translucent white-chalk.
Elara didn't look at him. She was looking at the sky, her eyes wide and featureless. "The light is so clean, Father," she whispered, her voice a hollow resonance. "I don't remember feeling heavy anymore."
"We have to go to the Shadows!" the father shouted, pointing toward the ruins of the 9th Bastion. But he realized there were no shadows. The Seraphim were standing on every rooftop, their wings vibrating in perfect unison.

Suddenly, the Beacon on the horizon pulsed. 
The father didn't scream. He **"Dulled."** His skin, hair, and clothes physically converted into a fine, white powder in three seconds. His Soul Core cracked like a glass bell, the energy being "Absorbed" by the Seraphim above.
Elara and her mother didn't even notice he was gone. They just continued to stand in the light, their own bodies slowly losing their definition. Within five minutes, the entire plaza was a featureless white-plain of ash, drifting through the silent, glass-walled streets.

The Seraph overseeing the plaza noted the event. "Sector 04-Clean. No inconsistencies remaining. Proceeding to the next grid."

 V. Aftermath: The Legacy of the White Ash
The 176th file records the official reclassification of the mortal races as **"Historical-Redundancies."** By the end of the Purge, the population of Eldrion had been reduced by 90%.

Archivist Torin’s Synthesis: The Purge of the 10th Race was the **"Execution of Complexity."** We are living in a world that has been "Format-Shifted." The Seraphim didn't just kill the people; they killed the *concept* of being human. 

Torin notes that the 176th file is the **"Final-Isolation"** of the **Hybrid King.** The King is the only being left who carries the "History" of the 10th race in his blood. He is the living archive of a deleted people. The 176th file is the record of the silence that waits for his first cry.

Final Word: 176 stands as the record of the day the light decided that the only way to be perfect was to be alone. The Silent Cities of Glass are now but humming reminders of the names we forgot to save.
