 167. The Grotesque Hordes: The Biological Entropy of the Abyssal Flesh

Classification: 2nd Holy War / Demonic Biology | Status: Canonical Record | Location: The 9th Dimension / The Iron Bastion | Primary Actors: Lord Malphas, Baron Valac, The 10th Race Survivors

 I. Biological Entropy: The Fusion Protocol and the Engineering of the Scourge
The **Grotesque Hordes** were not the result of procreation, but of **"Biological-Fusion."** During the 2nd Holy War, the Demon Lords realized that the 10 mortal races (the 10th race) were more useful as raw material than as slaves. Under the guidance of Baron Valac, the Progenitor of the Swarm, the Abyssal entities developed the **"Fusion Protocol."**

The protocol was a systematic application of raw Abyssal entropy to living biological tissue. In the presence of a Demon Lord’s static-field, the "Conceptual-Independence" of a soul was dissolved. This allowed the physical merging of multiple bodies, materials, and even industrial refuse into unified, multi-limbed entities of absolute violence. In technical terms, the hordes were **"Entropic-Gestalts"**—living machines whose only purpose was the physical deletion of the Monarchs' infrastructure.

The ecology of the horde was one of **"Metabolic-Parasitism."** A Scourge-unit did not eat; it "Absorbed" the ambient Aether of the dimension it occupied. This created a localized effect known as the **"Wither-Zone,"** where all plant life and high-frequency light would physically die within minutes of the horde’s arrival. The air in these zones was thick with the smell of wet iron and charcoal-grey static.

 II. The Anatomy of a Scourge-Unit: Technical Specifications
To maximize tactical efficiency, Baron Valac divided the grotesque hordes into three primary "Casts" of biological entropy.

1. **The Sludge-Vessels (Breach Units)**: These were the basic infantry of the horde. 
   - **Biological Spec**: Composed of the dissolved remains of 5-10 human-celestials, merged into a single, amorphous mass of translucent grey flesh. 
   - **Technical Spec**: They did not use weapons; they physically "Leaked" onto an enemy’s armor, the Abyssal-Draft in their skin eroding silver-steel within seconds. They were effectively **"Sentient-Acid-Bombs."**
2. **The Fused-Iron-Seraphs (Aerial Shock Troops)**: A grotesque mockery of the Valkyries. 
   - **Biological Spec**: Valkyrie cadavers reanimated and merged with industrial piping and charcoal-grey silt.
   - **Technical Spec**: Their wings were made of jagged sheets of Abyssal-Iron, vibrating at a frequency that physically "Muted" the Valkyries' own command-signals. Each Seraph carried a **"Static-Lance"** capable of piercing a Monarch’s protection-field.
3. **The Static-Walkers (Siege Units)**: Towering, multi-story entities built from the ruins of entire cities and siphoned souls. 
   - **Biological Spec**: A "Thive-Mind" of hundreds of merged consciousnesses, permanently screaming in a unified frequency of absolute-zero.
   - **Technical Spec**: Their primary weapon was the **"Entropy-Gaze"**—a beam of raw Abyssal static that could "Gray-Out" an entire fortress wall in a single pulse.

Technical Specifications: "The Hordes do not feel pain, for pain requires a unified nervous system. They are a collection of surviving reflexes, driven by the hunger of the King in the Dark. To kill a grotesque is to merely stop the static."

 III. The Siege of the 9th Bastion: Tactical Log
The most complete record of the Hordes' military efficacy was the **Siege of the 9th Bastion** in the 9th Dimension—the last standing industrial fortress of the Monarch Malthus.

The Mission Profile: **"Operation Iron-Eater"**
1. **The Deployment (Day 1)**: Baron Valac launched ten thousand Sludge-Vessels into the Bastion’s lower conduits. They did not attack the walls; they physically "Clogged" the aetheric-filters of the fortress, causing the internal life-support to suffer a **"Phase-Failure."**
2. **The Descent of the Seraphs (Day 2)**: As the Bastion’s light-shields dimmed, five thousand Fused-Iron-Seraphs descended from the purple-grey clouds. They bypassed the anti-air cannons by vibrating out-of-phase with the target-tracking magic. 
3. **The Wall-Collapse (Day 3)**: A single Static-Walker approached the primary gate. It did not strike the gate; it "Looked" at it. The silver-steel gate turned to grey dust in five seconds.

Tactical Log Commander Aeliana (Recording from orbit): "The 9th Dimension is gone. I am seeing the surface of the planet being physically 'Un-woven.' The Hordes are not marching; they are 'Flowing' over the cities like a sea of wet ash. Malthus’s legions are firing their Aether-cannons, but the grotesques are just absorbing the shots and becoming larger. The air in the Bastion is now 90% Abyssal-Silt. Extraction is impossible."

 IV. The Screams of the Sludge-Pit: The Cinematic Narrative
the horror of the "Conversion" process reached its peak in the **Conversion-Pits** located in the ruins of the 9th Bastion.

The Scene:
A group of ten mortal survivors—human celestials—clung to each other in the dark of a basement. Above them, the sound of the horde was a constant "Hum" of wet flesh and grinding iron.
Suddenly, the floor was physically "Deleted." Not destroyed—it simply became a hole of charcoal-grey static. 
They fell into the **Sludge-Pit**, a sea of translucent grey matter and shifting geometric limbs. They were not alone; hundreds of other mortals were already there, their bodies slowly "Merging" with the grey sea.

"Do not let go!" the leader, a young woman named Elara, shouted as the grey silt began to climb her legs.
But her hand turned to smoke. She looked down and saw that her fingers were Physically "Becoming" the hand of the man next to her. There was no blood, only a cold, vibrating pressure. Their thoughts began to bleed together. Elara could no longer remember her own name; she could only remember the hunger of the King in the Dark. 

A Fused-Iron-Seraph landed at the edge of the pit, its charcoal-grey wings casting a negative-shadow over the merging mortals. "Welcome to the Unity," the creature spoke, its voice a resonance of twelve different voices. "Your silence will finally be shared."

The ten survivors did not die. They became **Unit 094-S** of the grotesque horde—a single, multi-armed entity that spent the next hundred years erasing the world they had once tried to save.

 V. Aftermath: The Legacy of the Biological Void
The 167th file records the official reclassification of the mortal survivors as **"Refuse."** The Demon Lords had proven that life was just another industrial resource to be recycled.

Archivist Torin’s Synthesis: The Grotesque Hordes were the **"De-Personalization of Evil."** We are no longer fighting monsters; we are fighting the "Deleted-Versions" of ourselves. 

Torin notes that the Hordes are the "Primary-Waste-Product" that the **Hybrid King** must clean. The King is the only one who can "Un-fuse" the grotesques, giving the merged souls back their individual names before they are returned to the Aether. the 167th file is the record of the world’s broken family.

Final Word: 167 stands as the record of the day we learned that the Abyss does not hate us; it simply wants to use us as spare parts for the end of the world.
