 164. The Incursion of the First Dimension: The Graying of the Light

Classification: 2nd Holy War / Primordial Corruption | Status: Canonical Record | Location: The 1st Dimension / The Radiant Spires | Primary Actors: Lord Malphas, The Sun-Walkers (10th Race), Zenithos

---

 I. The First Dimension: The Light Singularity and the Architecture of Radiance
In the pre-war era, the **First Dimension** was the absolute zenith of the aetheric architecture. It was the original home of the Titan Luminaria and handled the "Energetic-Distribution" for all 13 worlds. It was not a world of earth and sky, but a **"Light-Singularity"**—a dimension where matter was composed of solid, high-frequency radiance. 

The ecology of the First Dimension was one of literal enlightenment. The inhabitants, known as the **Sun-Walkers** (the most advanced caste of the 10th mortal race), did not possess physical bodies in the traditional sense. They were entities of semi-transparent bioluminescence, their thoughts transmitted through light-pulses and their cities built from "Solidified-Intention." In the 1st Dimension, the concept of "Shadow" was a mathematical impossibility; every corner of the world was illuminated by the **Eternal-Radiance-Node** at the center of the world.

Zenithos and the Monarchs viewed the First Dimension as their most secure stronghold. It was the "Power-Plant" of their empire. They believed that because the Abyss was made of silence and darkness, it could never physically survive the intense, high-frequency environment of the Light-Singularity. They were wrong. They failed to realize that the Abyss does not fight light; it "Recycles" it.

 II. The Physics of Dark-Aether Corruption: Technical Specifications
The invasion of the First Dimension was orchestrated by **Lord Malphas (The First Demon Lord)**. He utilized a new form of technical warfare known as **"Phase-Inversion-Corruption."** Rather than attempting to extinguish the light of the 1st Dimension, Malphas introduced a virus of **"Dark-Aether."**

1. **The Negative-Harmonic**: Dark-Aether is standard Aether whose resonance has been shifted exactly 180 degrees out-of-phase. When Dark-Aether encounters Primordial Light, it does not create darkness; it creates **"Gray-Static."** This is a state of thermodynamic neutrality where the energy of the light is physically "Cancelled" but the matter remains.
2. **The Siphoning of Frequency**: The corruption process targeted the **Eternal-Radiance-Node**. By injecting Abyssal silt into the node’s core, Malphas converted the world’s power source into a "Siphon of Meaning." The light produced by the node began to "Eat" the history of whatever it touched.
3. **The Molecular-Graying**: As the Dark-Aether spread, the solid-light architecture of the 1st Dimension began to **"Decohere."** The radiant spires did not crumble; they turned into ash and charcoal-grey silt, losing their structural integrity while maintaining their original shape as a "Ghost-Architecture."

Technical Specifications: "The corruption is not a paint; it is a rewrite. It tells the atoms of the light-world that they are actually made of silence. The result is a 'Conceptual-Corpse'—a world that looks like a world but has the aetheric signature of a grave."

 III. The Fall of the Radiant Spires: Tactical Log
The military campaign for the 1st Dimension was designated **"Operation Blind-Light."** Lord Malphas led ten legions of **"Static-Shifters"** directly into the Radiant Spires.

The Mission Profile:
1. **The Breach of the Sun-Gate (Day 1)**: Malphas did not attack the gates. He utilized a **"Sub-Aetheric-Drill"** to bypass the light-shields. Once inside, the shifters began the systematic "Washing" of the city’s mana-reservoirs. 
2. **The Blinding of the Guardians**: The celestials who defended the spires attempted to use their **"Luminaria-Blades."** The shifters simply "Absorbed" the blades’ frequency, converting the celestial warriors into **"Frequency-Ghosts"** who could no longer see or interact with reality.
3. **The Seizing of the Node**: On the third day, Malphas reached the central Spire. He did not kill the Sun-Walker priests; he physically "Unhinged" their minds by exposing them to the raw, unrefined vacuum of the Abyss.

Tactical Log Malphas: "The light in this dimension is delicious. It is so pure it is brittle. My units are not even fighting; they are simply standing in the streets and allowing the radiance to 'Feed' them. For every photon the celestials manifest, we give them a thousand tons of grey. The Sun-Walkers are weeping, but their tears are turning to charcoal silt before they hit the ground."

 IV. The Blinding of the Sun-Walkers: The Cinematic Narrative
the fall of the 1st Dimension reached its terminal point at the **Apex of the Radiant Spires**, where the High-Priest Elias attempted a final mass-synchronization to save his people.

The Scene:
Elias stood at the edge of the Spire, his body a blinding white fire. Behind him, thousands of Sun-Walkers were gathered, their voices joined in a desperate, high-pitched song of order. They were trying to "Sing" the Abyss back into the Trench.
Lord Malphas stepped from the shadows. He did not look like a monster; he looked like a "Void-in-the-Air." He carried no weapon, only a small **"Blight-Sphere"** pulsing with charcoal-grey static.
"Your song is beautiful, Priest," Malphas spoke, his voice vibrating through the crystal floors. "But it is out of tune. You are singing to a God who was deleted three centuries ago."
"The Light is absolute!" Elias shouted, his eyes glowing with the fire of the original stars. "The dark cannot exist here!"

Malphas simply dropped the sphere. 
As it shattered, the sound did not propagate. Instead, the "Concept of Light" was physically removed from the room. Elias’s body did not catch fire; it **"Dulled."** His white-gold skin turned to a muddy grey. His eyes, once star-sized lenses of vision, became opaque, black-glass stones. 
Around him, the Sun-Walkers screamed, but the sound was "Eaten" by the grey-static in the air. They were not dying; they were **"Blinded."** Not just their eyes, but their souls. They could no longer remember what it felt like to be light. They stumbled through the grey-dust of their own cities, their hands reaching for a sun that was now a black hole in the sky.

Malphas watched them fall. "Now you are perfect," he whispered. "Now you are a blank canvas for our King."

 V. Aftermath: The Legacy of the Gray World
The 164th file records the official reclassification of the 1st Dimension as the **"Abyssal-Processing-Zone."** The light was gone, replaced by the industrial hum of the Demon Lords' machines.

Archivist Torin’s Synthesis: The Incursion of the First Dimension was the moment the Monarchs lost their power-source. By allowing the First Dimension to fall, Zenithos turned Eldrion into a **"Dying-Battery."** 

Torin notes that the Sun-Walkers who survived did not become demons; they became the **"Gray-Walkers"**—a mutation of the 10th race that exists in a state of permanent nihilism. They are the ones who will eventually recognize the **Hybrid King** as the "True Sun," the only being who can bridge the gap between the blinding light of the past and the absolute darkness of the present. The 164th file is the record of the world’s first eclipse that never ended.

Final Word: 164 stands as the record of the day the light learned to be afraid of its own shadow. The Radiant Spires are now but a monument to a god who forgot to close the door.
