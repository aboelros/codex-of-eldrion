---

 I. The Vertical Logistics

The 4th Holy War (The Apex Rebellion) was fundamentally different from any military campaign conceived in terrestrial history because it was not fought across horizontal geography. It was fought vertically. 

Following the signing of the Unification Pact (see: SYS_05/51_The_Unification_Pact.md), the mortal coalition established its centralized command within the deep equatorial craters of the 4th Dimension. Their objective was the Divine Apex, located in the 12th Dimension.

Moving a multi-species infantry force composed of millions of practitioners upward through eight distinct dimensions each possessing radically different baseline physics, gravity constraints, and atmospheric pressure was a logistical nightmare that bordered on the mathematically impossible. 

The strategy, drafted by Kaelen the Exiled God and executed by Theron, relied entirely on the Hub-Spoke model. 

The mortals did not slowly conquer the geography of each dimension. They ignored the civil centers and focused entirely on executing rapid, overwhelming kinetic strikes against the specialized Seraphic Frequency Gates connecting the dimensional plateaus. Once a gate was secured, the entire army was violently bottlenecked and pushed upward before Gabriel could establish a successful orbital bombardment.

---

 II. The Tactical Synergy

The brutal success of the March on the Heavens was attributed entirely to the perfect implementation of the Aetheric synergies permitted by the Hybrid King.

When the massive mortal column ascended into the 7th Dimension, they were met by four full legions of Seraphim operating in their preferred high-altitude, low-gravity environment. In the 3rd Holy War, this Seraphic advantage would have resulted in an absolute slaughter.

In the 4th Holy War, the mortals deployed the "Triple-Blind" formation:
1. Silas's Choirs (Acoustic Resonance) maintained a localized frequency-jamming dome extending three miles vertically, rendering Seraphic targeting telemetry utterly useless. 
2. Theron’s Chaos Engineers intentionally detonated raw thermal cores along the perimeter of the march, creating towering storms of Entropy that physically obscured the mortal infantry from Gabriel's long-range optical panopticon. 
3. Garrick’s Vanguard (Mass) acted as the physical battering ram. Operating under heavy Iron Born thermal shields, the Vanguard squads simply ignored standard Seraphic plasma infantry entirely, hurling themselves forward as momentum singularities specifically targeting the 8th Dimension gates.

At the exact center of this formation stood the Hybrid King, continuously funneling the ambient Mana generated by the three vastly different tactical wings so perfectly that none of the factions suffered from cross-frequency friction. The Seraphim did not know where the mortals were, could not hear their commands, and were being physically run over by troops generating localized black holes.

---

 III. The Mortality Rate and the Slog

Despite the terrifying tactical efficiency of the mortal column, the March on the Heavens was an absolute meat-grinder. 

Every dimensional gate breached by Garrick’s Vanguard required a significant sacrifice of human life. The hyper-dense gravitational charges eventually warped and snapped the spines of the Vanguard operatives. Silas’s Elven snipers frequently blew out their own vocal cords and shattered their own skulls maintaining the Acoustic dome for weeks at a time without sleeping. 

Gabriel’s Seraphim, incapable of fear, executed brutal, suicidal counter-strikes. Entire wings of Seraphim would intentionally overdrive their own Cores, executing atmospheric dives straight into Theron’s chaos storms simply to detonate as massive plasma bombs. 

The mortality rate of the mortal coalition crossing the 8th and 9th Dimensions hovered around 68%. The only reason the column continued to advance was the Covenant of Trust binding them. No lineage wanted to be the first to break the Unification Pact and abandon the others to Gabriel’s clean-up crews. 

---

 IV. The Arrival at the Periphery

After fourteen grueling, uninterrupted years of vertical combat, the surviving vanguard of the mortal coalition finally breached the upper gate of the 11th Dimension, placing them directly on the cosmological doorstep of the Divine Apex. 

The march had achieved what Zenithos believed inherently impossible: it had forced the entire Seraphic Order into a defensive, reactionary posture. The mortals had not just survived the vertical climb; they had successfully imported the brutal, messy physics of the dirt directly into the pristine airspace of the Gods. 

The stage was set for the final, ultimate kinetic clash to determine the sovereignty of the 13 Dimensions. The March had concluded. The final siege was about to begin.
