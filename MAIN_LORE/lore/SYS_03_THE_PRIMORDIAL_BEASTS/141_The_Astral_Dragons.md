 I. The Definition of a Primordial Beast

Before detailing the Astral Dragons, the archive must contextualize the category of "Primordial Beasts" (SYS_03). 

These entities are not animals in the conventional biological sense. They are not products of standard evolutionary paths dictated by Juno's genetic frameworks. The Primordial Beasts are spontaneously generated manifestations of the universe’s earliest physics. 

When the Titans were forging the 13 Dimensions, immense quantities of raw, unregulated Aether and Mana spilled outward from the Archon-Chaos collision. In areas where this energy pooled and stagnated, the universe attempted to self-organize the chaos into structure. The resulting structures were the Primordial Beasts massive, semi-sentient embodiments of physical laws, acting as the universe's immune system and regulatory maintenance crew.

They existed before the ten mortal races. They predate the Seraphic Order. They answer only to the deepest foundational frequencies of the cosmos.

---

 II. The Biology of the Astral Dragons

The Astral Dragons are the physical embodiment of dimensional stability and gravitational charting. 

In terms of scale, an adult Astral Dragon is not measured in feet or meters, but in atmospheric displacement. Historical records from the 1st Holy War describe fully matured specimens whose wingspans casting shadows that covered entire mountain ranges in the 5th Dimension.

Their biology is a hybrid of organic material and crystalized cosmic dust. Their "scales" are heavily compressed plates of raw meteoritic iron, fused with Dying Star substrate (the exact same material Ornn The Smith used to forge Holy Relics). Because their bodies are composed of such phenomenally dense material, they possess a localized gravitational pull. A flying Astral Dragon distorts the physics of the airspace around it, pulling loose debris, storms, and unanchored practitioners into its wake.

Crucially, Astral Dragons do not breathe fire. Fire is a terrestrial chemical reaction. An Astral Dragon exhales pure Aetheric plasma a beam of frictionless energy that does not burn, but rather disintegrates the atomic bonds of whatever it touches, returning the target to raw, unformatted Mana.

---

 III. The Cosmic Navigators

Prior to the Splintering of Dimensions, the primary function of the Astral Dragons was navigation and path-clearing. 

The space between the 13 Dimensions (the metaphysical "void" before it was weaponized by Shaders) was often clogged with chaotic energy storms left over from the Dawn Era. The Astral Dragons flew through these storms, absorbing the volatile energy into their own massive Soul Cores, leaving behind stabilized "tunnels" of calm frequency.

The early Celestial and Fae explorers mapped the dimensions entirely by following the migration paths of the Astral Dragons. Where a Dragon flew, reality was stable enough to transit. 

During the Titan censorship campaign led by the Monarchs, the Dragons were the first independent entities to be actively hunted by the Divine Apex. The Monarchs recognized that as long as the Dragons existed to clear paths between the dimensions, the ten races could never be truly isolated. To control inter-dimensional transit, the Monarchs had to control or eliminate the entities that naturally maintained the roads.

---

 IV. The Dark Age Status

The systematic hunting of the Astral Dragons during the 2nd and 3rd Holy Wars reduced their population from thousands to a handful of surviving specimens. Because they require astronomical amounts of ambient Mana to sustain their size, the Seraphic Dampeners installed during the Dark Age functionally poisoned their habitats.

The archive confirms the existence of at least three surviving Astral Dragons. However, they are currently in a state of Deep Hibernation. 

To survive the Mana starvation caused by the Seraphic Harvests, these Dragons have buried themselves deep within the planetary mantles of the 4th, 7th, and 9th Dimensions. They have slowed their Heart-Cores to a single beat every few decades, entering a state of petrified stasis. 

Highland miners in the 4th Dimension occasionally uncover massive, smooth veins of meteoritic iron that pulse with a faint, rhythmic Aetheric frequency. The resistance strictly enforces a ban on mining these veins. They are not ore deposits; they are the sleeping coils of the universe's first navigators. 

The resistance maintains a theoretical hope that if the Celestial Nexus is ever reopened and the Seraphic Dampeners are destroyed, the sudden influx of rich, unstifled Mana will awaken the sleeping Dragons. Until then, they remain entombed in the rock, waiting for the universe to breathe again.

 Archivist Note: The Sky We Lost
When you read the earliest poetry of the Fae and the Celestials, they do not write about the stars. They write about the shapes that moved between the stars. The sky was not an empty, terrifying void; it was an ocean populated by Leviathans and Dragons. The Monarchs didn't just steal our freedom; they stole our context. They emptied the sky to make us feel small, because a small population is easier to manage. Every time a strictly enforced mining ban is upheld in the deep tunnels, we are preserving the promise that the sky might one day be full again.
