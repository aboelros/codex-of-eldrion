 I. The Definition of a Relic

In standard military vernacular, any weapon manipulated with Aether is casually referred to as a "magic weapon" or an "enhanced blade." This is a fundamental misunderstanding of the physics involved. A sword swung by a practitioner pushing Aether into the steel is just a sword conducting energy, much like a copper wire conducting electricity. Once the practitioner stops channeling the energy, the sword is inert.

A Holy Relic is completely fundamentally different. A Relic is not a conduit. A Relic is a battery, an amplifier, and a semi-sentient participant in the practitioner's Soul Core loop. 

A true Relic does not go inert when dropped. It maintains its own internal Aetheric resonance, possessing a localized magnetic, thermal, or kinetic field that operates entirely independent of a wielder. 

The creation of these artifacts represents the absolute zenith of physical engineering and Aetheric theory, a discipline pioneered during the 2nd Holy War and perfected by the legendary Iron Born smith, Ornn, prior to his disappearance.

 III. Resonance and Rejection

Because a Holy Relic houses a sliver of a host's Soul Core, it is effectively biologically locked to that host's frequency. When the original wielder wields it, the weapon requires zero energetic cost to activate. It is an extension of their arm. 

The complication arises when a Relic changes hands. 

If a practitioner dies, the sliver inside the weapon does not die with them. It remains trapped in the metal, holding a static echo of the dead practitioner's frequency. If a new practitioner attempts to wield the Relic, their Soul Core must interact with the trapped sliver. 

This interaction is called Resonance.

If the frequencies clash, the result is Rejection. The weapon will violently repel the new user's Aether. It will burn their hands, drastically increase its own physical weight to become unwieldy, or emit a piercing acoustic feedback loop. The archive has records of Seraphic squads capturing highly attuned resistance Relics, only to have the weapons detonate in their armories because the Seraphic frequency aggressively clashed with the trapped mortal soul.

 IV. The Attuned Weapons and the Seraphic Ban

The culmination of Relic theory occurred during the 4th Holy War, through the collaboration of Silas (Sentinel of Resonance) and Ornn. 

Silas deduced the precise frequency of the Seraphic biology, which had previously rendered them invulnerable to mortal strikes. Ornn utilized the Dying Star methodology to forge Relics that were physically hard-coded to perfectly match that Seraphic frequency. 

These were the Attuned Weapons. They bypassed Resonance and Rejection entirely by functioning as tuning forks. When they struck a Seraph, the weapon matched the Seraph's exact frequency, sliding through their natural defenses like water through a sieve, before unleashing the stored kinetic energy directly against the Seraph's internal organs.
