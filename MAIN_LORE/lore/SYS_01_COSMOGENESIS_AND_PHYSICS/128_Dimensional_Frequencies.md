 I. The Fundamental Resonance

The 13 Dimensions are not separate physical spaces layered on top of each other, separated by walls or distance. They are co-located in the same physical volume of the universe. The separation between them is not spatial, but vibratory. 

Dimensional Frequency is the specific, foundational Aetheric resonance that defines the baseline "reality" of a given dimension. It determines the speed at which molecules vibrate, the density of ambient Mana, the specific physics of gravitational pull, and the operational ceilings of biological entities existing within that frame.

To exist in the 2nd Dimension is not to be physically lower or deeper than the 5th Dimension. It is to be attuned to the specific energetic wavelength that defines the 2nd Dimension's physical reality. 

If an entity's internal Aetheric resonance does not match the Dimensional Frequency of the space they occupy, they experience Dimensional Resistance. This manifests initially as fatigue, disorientation, and restricted access to external Mana. The Titans Lithos (Sculptor of Mass) and Cryos (Enforcer of Stasis) engineered this system to allow for infinite variation within finite space. By assigning different baseline frequencies, they created discrete evolutionary sandboxes.

 II. Inter-Dimensional Transit (Pre-Dark Age)

Before the Splintering of Dimensions that followed the 4th Holy War, transit between the dimensions was functionally possible through specialized application of Covenant binding and Aetheric manipulation. 

There were two primary methods of transit:

1. The Nexus Transit: The easiest and most common form of travel was simply physically walking to the Celestial Nexus, where the frequencies were mechanically synced by the Nexus's architecture. The traveler experienced a smooth, mathematically controlled gradient shift. This required zero Aetheric expenditure from the traveler.

2. Frequency Tuning (The Resonance Walk): For high-level practitioners, it was possible to artificially tune one's own Soul Core frequency to match a neighboring dimension. By compressing or expanding their internal Aether, a practitioner could match the target dimension's "pitch" closely enough to slip through the boundary. 

This required immense precision. Overshooting the frequency could catapult the practitioner into a hostile dimension, or worse, into the Abyssal frequency where standard physics broke down entirely. 

The Fae and the Celestials were known to have a natural biological aptitude for Frequency Tuning, allowing their cavalry and scouts to slip vertically through the dimensional layers with minimal effort, a tactic Elara The Swift heavily refined.

 IV. Aetheric Practice as Frequency Manipulation

Every martial or magical tradition in the Dark Age is, on a sub-atomic level, an exercise in localized frequency manipulation.

- Pyromancy: Temporarily escalating the ambient frequency of atmospheric molecules until they ignite.
- Gravitational Arts: Severely depressing the frequency of one's own mass relative to the environment.
- Acoustic Resonance: Generating Aetheric shockwaves by forcing two incompatible frequencies to crash into each other within a confined space.

Silas, the Sentinel of Resonance, based his entire 22-year research project on this understanding. He deduced that Seraphim were invulnerable to standard mortal attacks because they existed at a slightly elevated, out-of-phase frequency even while standing in standard dimensional space. A mortal sword pass through them harmlessly because the sword's frequency and the Seraph's frequency did not interact.

Silas's attuned weapons were designed to temporarily shift their own frequency upon impact, aligning perfectly with the Seraphic wavelength for exactly one micro-secondâ€”long enough to impart lethal kinetic energy.

 Archive Note on the 13th Dimension
The frequencies of Dimensions 1 through 11 are documented and charted. The 12th Dimension (The Divine Apex) is a closely guarded secret, its frequency heavily classified. The 13th Dimension remains the great theoretical blank space in the archive's physics texts. It is the Origin Point, the location of the Archon-Chaos collision. To tune to the 13th Dimension would theoretically mean vibrating at the exact frequency of creation and destruction simultaneously. There is no biological entity known to the archive capable of surviving such a state. It is widely hypothesized that the Titans were sealed here, crushed under the weight of the universe's baseline vibration.
