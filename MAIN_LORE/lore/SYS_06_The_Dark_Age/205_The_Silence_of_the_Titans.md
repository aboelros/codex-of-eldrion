This archive categorically refutes this narrative. 

The Titans are not dead. They are in a state of **"Enforced-Thermodynamic-Stasis."** Following the conclusion of the **1st Holy War** (see: 153), Zenithos and Gabriel realized they could not physically destroy the Titans without triggering the total **"Un-winding"** of universal mass. The Titans’ only coherent survival response was to drop their Aetheric output to absolute zero and hold it there. They dug into the impenetrable foundational substrate of reality and stopped "Breathing." They are still there, in the silence, waiting for a frequency they can safely answer.
