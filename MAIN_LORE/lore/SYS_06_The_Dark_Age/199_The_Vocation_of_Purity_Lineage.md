 I. The Vocation and the Law: the Founding of the Secret Priesthood
The **Vocation of Purity** is the primary spiritual and genetic preservation network of the Dark Age. Founded by the survivors of the **Battle of the 9th Bastion** (see: 198), the Vocation was established as a direct response to the **Seraphic Crusade’s** attempt to "Simplify" the mortal soul.

While the primary resistance (The Wardens of the Railroad) focuses on military and logistical survival, the Vocation focuses on the **"Purity-of-the-Code."** They are a secret lineage of scholars, medics, and priests who have dedicated their lives to the clandestine cultivation of Tier-3 and Tier-4 Aetheric talent. They believe that the human soul is not just a biological error, but a **"Symphonic-Record"** of the Titans' original intent.

The Vocation operates under the **"Law of the Silent Flame."** Members do not practice magic for battle or utility; they practice magic for **"Integration."** Every initiate is trained to maintain a perfectly unified, multi-spectral frequency that mirrors the **Hybrid King's** own architecture. This allows them to act as biological "Archives," preserving the high-frequency potential of Eldrion until the day of the final awakening.

Technical Specifications: "Purity is not a moral state; it is a 'Frequency-Standard.' We are keeping the gears of the soul clean in a world made of sand. One inconsistency in the sync, and the Dampener catches you. But if you are pure... you are the sand."
