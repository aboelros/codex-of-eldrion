 I. What Corrupted Aether Becomes

When a mortal dies by violence in a high-Aetheric-intensity context   a battle, a mass Will Severance event, the immediate aftermath of a Mana harvesting surge   the Soul Core's collapse does not always produce clean dissolution. A Soul Core in full defensive posture at the moment of biological death, denied the gradual dissolution pathway that natural death allows, can shatter rather than dissolve. The resulting shattered Soul Core material   Aetheric fragments of specific corrupted frequency, carrying the emotional and intentional residue of the death's specific character   does not dissipate immediately.

It contaminates.

The contamination operates through the Mana field: shattered Soul Core fragments, expelled into the ambient Mana field at the specific frequency of the violence that produced them, alter the local Mana field's resonance characteristics toward the frequency of destruction, trauma, and severed biological will. A Mana field with sufficient corrupted Aether contamination begins producing, in new biological entities whose Soul Cores develop within its resonance, a specific developmental bias toward the violence frequency.

New biological entities developing in a contaminated Mana field are statistically more prone to Soul Core architectures that default to destructive Aetheric expression. Not inevitably   the bias is statistical, not deterministic. But the contamination produces, over generations, a slow shift in the Mana field's effect on Soul Core development in the affected zone.

And contamination creates more contamination. The entities that develop with the destruction bias are more likely to die in the conditions that produce shattered Soul Cores. Which expels more corrupted fragments. Which increases the contamination.

Without intervention, a sufficiently contaminated Mana field zone becomes an escalating contamination source. Left alone, it becomes the geological origin point of high-Grotesque-prevalence. Left longer, it becomes an Abyss-gradient zone   a region where the local Mana field's frequency has shifted toward the Abyssal substrate's characteristics, creating conditions under which Abyssal entities can exist without crossing the Chain boundary.

The Vocation of Purity was created to prevent this.

 II. Diane the First: The Context of Her Creation

The original Diane was not named by the resistance archive. She is referred to as the First Purity because she was the practitioner in whom the Vocation's specific ritual methodology was first successfully systematized   the individual who, in the immediate aftermath of the 2nd Holy War's Abyssal Incursion, developed the specific Aetheric cleansing protocol that transformed battlefield Mana field contamination from an unmanaged escalating problem into a managed, addressable one.

The 2nd Holy War's Abyssal Incursion had produced the highest single-event Mana field contamination level in the eleven dimensions' documented history. The Demon Lords' forces   entities of Abyssal origin whose presence in the dimensional frequency space produced corrupted Aether expulsion even without combat deaths   combined with the extraordinary mortal and coalition casualty rates of the Incursion's containment campaign, had left contaminated Mana field zones across seven of the eleven dimensions.

The Gods, immediately following the Incursion's resolution through the Ashborn Battery Ritual, had addressed the contamination threat in the specific way Gods address problems: they had identified it, assessed that it required management, and determined that appropriate entities to manage it were the mortal practitioners who had the most relevant Aetheric capability.

The Vocation of Purity was assigned. Not volunteered for   assigned, in the specific sense that the Gods' designation of a function to mortal practitioners had in the period before the 4th Holy War. The first Diane had been identified as a practitioner with the specific Aetheric sensitivity profile that the contamination assessment protocol required, and had been designated for the Vocation.

She could refuse. The Gods could not compel her. But the Mana field zones were contaminating. Refusing was choosing the contamination.

She accepted.

 III. The Ritual and Its Cost

The specific cleansing ritual that the First Purity developed   working from the theoretical knowledge that Juno's biological specifications had provided and from the specific Aetheric mechanics of contamination that she had to deduce partly from observation of what the contamination was doing   was a process of active Mana field resonance correction.

The corrupted fragments in the Mana field had a specific resonance frequency: the frequency of the violence that had produced them. The cleansing ritual worked by introducing the inverse resonance   the specific counter-frequency that, sustained at sufficient amplitude over sufficient time, could disrupt the corrupted fragments' coherence and allow the Mana field to reabsorb them at neutral frequency.

This was the theory. The practice produced a specific problem that the First Purity documented in the first entry of the Vocation's record   preserved in the resistance archive through the chain of handoff documentation that each bearer of the Vocation had maintained:

"The counter-frequency must be produced from inside the contaminated zone to reach the fragments at the necessary amplitude. To produce the counter-frequency from inside the contaminated zone is to maintain one's own Soul Core resonance against the contamination field's pressure for the full duration of the cleansing. The contamination's effect on the practitioner inside the zone is not instantaneous   it is cumulative. Each session of cleansing work adds a specific load of counter-frequency exposure to the practitioner's Soul Core architecture. This load is not fully dischargeable between sessions. It accumulates."

She had described, in her first entry, the mechanism by which the Vocation would eventually kill its bearer. The cleansing work that prevented the Mana field from developing escalating contamination did so by transferring a portion of that contamination burden into the practitioner who performed it.

She had performed the work anyway, for forty-seven years, until the cumulative load produced the specific Soul Core architectural failure that the load trajectory had always predicted.

She had known this at the beginning. The first entry says she knew.

 IV. The Handoff and the Lineage

Before the cumulative load reached the specific failure threshold   before the architectural damage was irreversible   the First Purity had identified, trained, and completed the handoff of the Vocation's methodology to a designated successor.

The handoff was not simply a transfer of knowledge. It was a transfer of the specific Aetheric frequency profile that the Vocation's ongoing work required: the successor needed, from the moment the handoff was complete, to maintain the counter-frequency resonance that kept the cleansed zones from re-contaminating. The handoff had to be crisp. Overlapping was not possible   two practitioners could not simultaneously maintain the resonance at the architecture level the Vocation required.

The successor took the Vocation at the moment the First Purity released it. This is documented in the handoff record as a transfer of approximately nineteen seconds   the specific window between one practitioner's resonance release and another's resonance assumption.

Nineteen seconds in which the cleansed zones were without their steady-state maintenance.

The archive notes that nineteen seconds of unmaintained time across the cleansed zones produced a measurable contamination increase in seven of the twelve highest-priority zones. Seven contamination increments from nineteen seconds of predecessor-to-successor transition.

Every handoff since has had the same nineteen-second gap. Every handoff has produced the same seven contamination increments. The First Purity knew this would happen and documented it, and every subsequent bearer of the Vocation has known this would happen and performed the handoff anyway, because the alternative to nineteen seconds of contamination increase was permanent contamination escalation.

The Vocation has passed through approximately thirty bearers   the archive's count is uncertain because the middle Dark Age period's handoff records were partially lost in Zephon's censorship sweeps. The lineage is documented at both ends and is partially missing in the middle.

Each bearer was identified, trained, designated, and then carried the Vocation until the cumulative load required the choice between handoff and collapse. Each one handed off rather than collapsed, because collapse without handoff meant no successor, which meant no maintenance, which meant the contamination.

The modern Diane   Kevin's era's bearer   is the inheritor of this lineage. The archive does not know how far the cumulative load has developed in her specific case. The archive knows the trajectory that the Vocation's specific mechanism produces. It monitors.
