 42. The Labyrinth of Mirrors

Description: A shifting, non-Euclidean maze of silver-glass and dark-matter located in the "Refractive Belt" of the 5th Dimension, where reality is as thin as a reflection.

---

 Overview
The Labyrinth of Mirrors is the primary defense system for the North-Western quadrant of the 5th Dimension. It is not a structure in the conventional sense, but a "Spatial Filter" a multi-tiered maze made of "Aetheric-Silver-Glass." The mirrors in the labyrinth do not show a person their physical reflection; instead, they show "Possibilities." One mirror might show the person as they were a hundred years ago, while another might show a future where the Divine Apex has collapsed.

To navigate the Labyrinth is a trial of mental fortitude. The constant shifting of the mirrors and the overlapping reflections of thousands of timelines create a world of "Infinite Glare" and deeper shadows. It is a place where many seekers of truth have been lost, their identities slowly dissolving until they become nothing more than another reflection within the silver halls.

 Geography & Architecture
The Labyrinth is a non-Euclidean landscape of Refractive Corridors.

1. The Hall of Memories: The outermost layer of the maze, where the mirrors show scenes from the traveler's own past. This is the least dangerous but most emotionally taxing area.
2. The Prism-Plazas: Open chambers where the light of the Apex is split into thousands of different colors, creating localized anomalies where gravity or time may be different.
3. The Mirror-Core: The central chamber where the mirrors become perfectly transparent, allowing a person to look through them into other dimensions or into the Void itself.

The architecture is "Refractive-Gothic" high, arched ceilings made of silver-glass, floors of polished obsidian that reflect upward, and walls that shift positions every twelve minutes to ensure no map can ever be accurate.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Oracle, the Labyrinth was a small temple used by the dimension’s seers to predict the shifts of the Astral Sea. During the Era of the Great Distortion, Warden Noctis ordered the temple to be "Expanded to the Horizon." He wanted a prison that was made of "Truth and Lies," where the enemies of the Apex could be lost in their own potential futures.

The Labyrinth is the site of the Shattering of the Oracle, an event where the last independent seer of the 5th Dimension supposedly broke a "Master Mirror," causing a fragment of the "End-of-Eldrion" to be permanently trapped within the maze. Legend says that those who can find this fragment will see the exact moment the 4th Holy War began and how it can be undone.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Mirror-Phantoms are the primary inhabitants. They are not ghosts, but "Reflection-Remnants" beings created when a person stays in the labyrinth for too long and their reflection becomes more solid than their physical body. They are translucent and silent, eternally repeating the actions they were performing when they became "Lost."

The Seekers of Reality are a group of the resistance who believe that the "True-Eldrion" is hidden at the center of the maze. They use "Reflection-Damping" goggles and heavy-duty blindfolds to navigate the labyrinth by touch alone, avoiding the seductive pull of the mirrors.

The Warden’s Mirror-Kin are specialized guards who can "Step through the Glass." They use the labyrinth as a series of portals, allowing them to appear behind intruders and drag them into the "Inner-Reflections" from which there is no escape.

 Dangers & Hazards
Identity-Dissolution is the primary psychological hazard. The more a person looks into the mirrors of the labyrinth, the more they begin to doubt which version of themselves is real. Eventually, the person’s physical form will "Snap," turning them into another Mirror-Phantom.

Spatial Distortion is a physical hazard. The labyrinth is non-Euclidean. A person may follow a straight corridor for hours and emerge exactly where they started, or they might turn a corner and find themselves walking on the ceiling.

Reflective-Beams are an automated defense. Certain mirrors are designed to catch and focus the twilight of the dimension into "Flash-Beams" that can temporarily paralyze a person or even "Bleach" the color from their skin and eyes.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the labyrinth is the True Face. It is rumored that at the very heart of the Mirror-Core, there is a single mirror that shows neither the past nor the future, but the "Absolute-Present." Legend says that anyone looking into this mirror will see the True Form of Gabriel, and that the sight is so terrifying that it either shatters the person’s mind or gives them the power to destroy an Angel with a single thought.

Another rumor suggests that the Labyrinth of Mirrors is not a prison. Some claim it is a "Training-Ground for the 10th Dimension (The Dream-Realm)," and that the Warden is using the reflections to "Stress-Test" the mental stability of his most elite guards before they are sent to the deeper dimensions.
