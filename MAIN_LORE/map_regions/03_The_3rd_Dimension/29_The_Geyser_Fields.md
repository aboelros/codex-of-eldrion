 29. The Geyser Fields

Description: A sprawling volcanic plain on the seafloor characterized by thousands of active geysers that spew super-heated water and minerals into the Boiling Sea.

---

 Overview
The Geyser Fields are the 3rd Dimension’s primary energy source and its most volatile landscape. Located in the "Tectonic Rift" that bisects the seafloor, this region is a chaotic garden of white smoke and orange fire. Thousands of mineral-rich geysers erupt rhythmically from the earth, sending plumes of water hot enough to melt lead miles into the dark ocean above. 

The air within the Geyser Fields (captured in diving helmets) is a lethal cocktail of sulfur and steam. The water is opaque, filled with swirling clouds of metallic dust and "Hydrothermal Snow" particles of cooling mineral that fall like a silent, grey blizzard. It is a place of primal force, where the boundary between the world’s core and the ocean is a thin, fragile layer of basalt.

 Geography & Architecture
The geography is defined by Eruptive Columns and Basalt Slabs.

1. The Grand Vent: The largest geyser in the dimension, which erupts every hour with the force of a million cannons, creating a "Thermal Pillar" that reaches the boiling surface.
2. The Sulfur-Pans: Shallow depressions filled with bright yellow, liquid sulfur that creates a sharp contrast against the black volcanic rock.
3. The Chimney-Forests: Clusters of tall, thin mineral towers formed by the constant precipitation of minerals from the geysers. Some of these towers rise over five hundred feet.

The architecture is entirely industrial: Heat-Capture Domes made of reinforced glass and carbon-steel that sit directly over the smaller geysers, harvesting the steam for the dimension’s power grid.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the Blue, the Geyser Fields were a series of gentle, warm springs used by the Mer-folk as healing spas. During the Great Tapping, Gabriel ordered the deep-drilling of the seafloor to ignite the core’s full potential. This fractured the tectonic plates, turning the gentle springs into the violent geyser fields that exist today.

Legend says that the geysers are the "Breath of the Old Warden," who was buried beneath the field following his rebellion. Every time a geyser erupts, it is said his spirit is attempting to scream a warning to the people of the Domes. The largest geyser, the Grand Vent, is whispered to be his literal mouth, eternally silenced by the weight of the sea.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Geyser-Engineers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a tough, stoic group of workers who live in mobile, armored "Crawler-Cities" that move across the basalt slabs to avoid the shifting eruption points. They are experts in pressure-control and thermal-dynamics.

The Heat-Strider Crabs are the dominant wildlife. These are massive, six-legged crustaceans with shells made of overlapping iron-plates. They feed on the minerals spewed by the geysers and are resistant to temperatures that would boil any other organism.

There is a faction known as the Steam-Kings. These are rogue engineers who have successfully hijacked several heat-capture domes. They selling "Black-Market Steam" to the resistance cells, using the geysers' energy to power illegal weaponry and communication arrays.

 Dangers & Hazards
Sudden Eruption is the most immediate hazard. The geysers follow a rhythm, but "Micro-Quakes" can cause a vent to erupt early. Being caught in a direct blast of a geyser means instant vaporization, even inside a reinforced diving hardsuit.

Sulfurous Clouds are a chemical threat. The geysers don't just spew water; they release high concentrations of sulfuric acid. This acid eats through diving suits and seals over time, turning the environment into a slow-acting trap.

Tectonic Shifts are a physical hazard. The ground in the Geyser Fields is constantly moving. Fissures can open beneath a traveler’s feet or a mountain of basalt can suddenly rise, crushing anyone in the path.

 Secrets & Rumors
The most well-kept secret of the fields is the Steam-Gate. It is rumored that at the very heart of the Grand Vent, the pressure and heat have created a permanent "Rift in Space." Legend says that if a traveler can enter the center of the geyser’s plume, they will be transported not to the surface, but directly to the 4th Dimension (The 4th Realm).

Another rumor suggests that the Geyser-Engineers are not actually working for the Warden. Some claim they have found a way to "Harmonize the Eruptions," and that they are planning to trigger a synchronized blast that will create a "Thermal Spear" powerful enough to reach up through the Boiling Sea and pierce the Golden Blockade itself.
