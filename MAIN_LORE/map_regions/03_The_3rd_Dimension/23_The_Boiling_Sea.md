 23. The Boiling Sea

Description: A vast, planet-spanning ocean of super-heated water, fueled by the intense volcanic activity of the 3rd Dimension’s core.

---

 Overview
The Boiling Sea is a world of steam and salt, a hydrothermal abyss that serves as the foundation of the 3rd Dimension. Unlike the crystalline oceans of the Apex, this sea is a churning, violent entity, its surface constantly obscured by a mile-thick layer of "Scalding Fog." The water temperature at the surface rarely drops below eighty degrees Celsius, while in the deeper trenches, the pressure keeps the water liquid at temperatures that would melt lead.

To the survivors of the 3rd Dimension, the Sea is both a provider and a predator. The heat generated by the sea powers their cities, but the ocean itself is highly corrosive and filled with predators that have adapted to the extreme thermal environment. It is a place of permanent twilight, where the only light comes from the glowing vents of the seafloor or the bioluminescence of the deep-sea terrors.

 Geography & Architecture
The geography of the 3rd Dimension is defined by its Thermal Columns.

1. The Steaming Surface: A turbulent layer where the water meets the atmosphere, characterized by massive "Steam-Tornadoes" that can lift a ship and toss it miles away.
2. The Hydrothermal Plains: Vast, underwater deserts of black sand and basalt, dotted with thousands of "Smokers" vents that spew out mineral-rich, super-heated water.
3. The Glass-Cliffs: Submerged mountain ranges formed from rapidly cooled lava. These cliffs are as transparent as the Sea of Glass but are riddled with razor-sharp edges and hollow "Bubble-Caves."

Architecture in the sea is largely subterranean or "Floating." The Thermal-Bells are bell-shaped cities anchored to the seafloor, kept inflated by the hot gases rising from the vents.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Tide, the 3rd Dimension was a world of temperate, blue oceans and lush islands. However, during the Great Warming, Gabriel ordered the Warden to accelerate the geothermal extraction of the core to provide energy for the Aetheric Forges. This caused a massive, dimension-wide volcanic eruption that sank every island and turned the oceans into the boiling abyss seen today.

The War of the Waves saw the last of the surface-dwelling civilizations attempt to build floating fortresses. Gabriel’s response was to "Un-anchor" the seafloor, triggering a series of tsunamis that wiped out ninety percent of the population. Now, the dimension is a graveyard of many civilizations, all resting beneath miles of scalding water.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Steam-Striders are the primary angelic presence. They are long-limbed, spider-like entities that can walk on the surface of the boiling water using surface-tension-manipulation. They serve as the Warden’s tax collectors, visiting the thermal-bells to ensure the "Salt-Tribute" is paid.

The Scalded are the humanoid descendants of the survivors. Their skin has become thick, leathery, and resistant to heat, often appearing a deep red or purple. They live in the thermal-bells, surviving on a diet of deep-sea mollusks and "Thermal-Plankton."

There is a rumor of a faction known as the Abyssal Remnant. This is a group of ancient, pre-Warming scientists who have retreated into the deepest trenches. They have supposedly built a city made of "Living Kelp" that is immune to the heat, where they are culturing a virus that can "Cool the World."

 Dangers & Hazards
Scalding Currents are the most common threat. A sudden shift in the ocean’s thermal layers can send a wave of 150-degree water through a settlement, killing everyone instantly.

Mineral-Blindness is caused by the thick clouds of metallic dust spewed out by the Smokers. The dust is highly abrasive and can sear the eyes of those without "Glass-Lenses."

The Pressure-Crush is a physical reality for anyone attempting to dive into the deeper trenches. Without a "Hydraulic-Exoskeleton," a mortal’s body will be compacted into a ball the size of a fist within seconds of passing the one-mile mark.

 Secrets & Rumors
The most well-guarded secret of the Boiling Sea is the Sunken Sovereign. It is rumored that the original Warden of the 3rd Dimension, who refused Gabriel’s order to warm the core, was not executed. Instead, he was turned into a statue of solid gold and dropped into the deepest part of the sea. It is said that he is still alive, and if he is ever recovered, the water will turn cold at his touch.

Another rumor suggests that the Smokers are not natural vents. Some claim they are the Exhaust-Pipes of a Gargantuan Machine that Gabriel has built at the center of the world a machine that is preparing to "Flash-Freeze" the entire 3rd Dimension to turn it into a new foundation for the Divine Apex.
