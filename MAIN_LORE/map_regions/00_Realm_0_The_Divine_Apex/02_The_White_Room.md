 02. The White Room

Description: An infinite expanse of sensory erasure where the "impure" are processed.

---

 Overview
The White Room is not a room in any conventional sense; it is a psychological dimension, a sub-layer of the Divine Apex designed to serve as the ultimate "cleansing" facility for those who have defied the Order of Gabriel. It is an environment of absolute, uncompromising uniformity. There is no sky, no floor, and no horizon only a seamless, pearlescent glow that emanates from every direction at once.

To be sent to the White Room is to be sentenced to the erasure of the soul. Without color, sound, or physical landmarks to anchor the mind, the human psyche begins to cannibalize itself within hours. It is here that the resistance’s strongest warriors are turned into the Divine Apex’s most loyal servants, not through torture, but through the sheer weight of nothingness.

 Geography & Architecture
The architecture of the White Room is defined by its lack of features. It is estimated to be infinite, though most who enter never travel more than a few miles before their sense of direction collapses. There are no shadows, as the light comes from the "walls" themselves which are actually layers of solidified "Anti-Sin," a substance that absorbs all personal identity and reflects only the collective will of the Apex.

The only "structures" in this realm are the Cleansing Cubes monolithic, transparent blocks that periodically descend from the "ceiling" to envelop subjects. Inside these cubes, the atmospheric pressure is increased until the subject’s very thoughts are forced into a rhythmic, mechanical pattern. Occasionally, one might find a "Pillar of Purity," a single vertical line of silver that serves as the only point of reference in a millions-of-square-mile void.

 History & Lore
The White Room was established during the Second Era of Refinement. Gabriel realized that traditional execution only created martyrs, which in turn fueled the fires of the Splintered Dimensions. To solve this, he commissioned the Archangel of Logic to build a place where the "idea" of a rebel could be deleted without the messy necessity of death.

In the annals of the Divine Apex, the White Room is referred to as "The Great Mercy." It is the place where the "Discordant" are made "Harmonious." History records that over ten thousand resistance leaders have entered the White Room; not one has ever left with their original name or purpose intact. They emerge as "Hollows" perfect, quiet citizens with eyes like polished marble.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The primary inhabitants are the Scrubbed. These are individuals caught in the middle of their "de-fragmentation" process. They wander the white void aimlessly, often dressed in simple, pale robes that blend into the environment. They do not speak, for they have forgotten language, but they move in eerie, synchronized patterns, as if sensing a beat only they can hear.

The overseers of this realm are the Censors. Unlike the armored soldiers of the Radiant Bastion, the Censors are tall, spindly figures wrapped in bandages made of "Light-Linen." They move without walking, gliding across the white floor like needles on a record. Their job is to ensure that no "mental noise" imagination, grief, or defiance persists within the subjects.

 Dangers & Hazards
The greatest hazard is Sensory Psychosis. Within six hours, a mortal will begin to hallucinate colors that aren't there. Within twelve, they will lose the ability to distinguish their own body from the floor. By the twenty-four-hour mark, the "White-Out" is complete; the self is gone.

There is also the threat of the Dead-Zones. These are areas where the light becomes so concentrated it acts as a physical weight, pinning subjects to the ground and "pressing" the memories out of their skulls. Survivors of the White Room describe a sensation of their brain being "washed with cold acid" whenever they try to remember their families or their homes.

 Secrets & Rumors
There is a persistent rumor among the "Scrubbed" who still retain a fragment of mind about the Singular Stain. It is said that somewhere in the infinite white, there is a single black dot on the floor the only imperfection in the entire realm. Legends say this dot is the congealed blood of the first rebel, and if a subject can find it and touch it, their memories will return in a violent, protective rush.

Some also whisper that the White Room is actually the "Stomach" of the Divine Apex, and that the mental energy stripped from the inhabitants is what powers the Aetheric Forges where the angelic armors are made.
