 72. The Petrified Forest

Description: A gargantuan expanse of prehistoric arboreal structures where time has been artificially brought to a total halt, turning all organic matter into seamless, indestructible stone.

---

 Overview
The Petrified Forest is the most visually static and eerily quiet region of the 10th Dimension a realm defined by the decay of time and the preservation of loss. Rising three hundred feet into a permanent, grey-mist sky, the forest consists of thousands of "Stasis-Oaks" and "Memory-Firs" whose biological processes were frozen eons ago. Every detail, from the intricate patterns on the bark to the individual veins in the frozen leaves, has been perfectly preserved in a state of colorless, white-and-grey stone.

The atmosphere in the forest is one of absolute, suffocating stillness. There is no wind, no sound of animals, and the air itself feels "Heavy," as if it too is struggling against the dimension’s temporal-anchors. To enter the forest is to step out of the flow of history. It is a place of mourning and scientific curiosity, where the Warden preserves the "Biological-Blueprints" of the worlds Gabriel has destroyed.

 Geography & Architecture
The Forest is a landscape of Vertical Statues and Mist-Valleys.

1. The Grove of Stillness: The central, most dense part of the forest, where the stone trees are so close together that they form a seamless roof of petrified leaves.
2. The Ash-Basins: Circular depressions between the trees where the "Dust of Time" the pulverized remains of the dimension’s original soil collects in deep, white drifts.
3. The Static-Rivers: Former waterways that have been frozen into solid, translucent paths of "Time-Ice."

There is no modern architecture. The inhabitants the Still-Walkers live in Tree-Hollows that have been ground out of the petrified trunks using acoustic-drills.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Breath, the 10th Dimension was a world of rapid growth and vibrant life. During the Great Stasis, Warden Jael decided that the dimension’s inhabitants were "Aging too quickly to be Useful." He activated a series of "Chrono-Anchors" that siphoned the time-energy from the region, resulting in the instantaneous petrification of everything within five hundred miles.

History records the Lament of the First Leaf, a story about the last living tree in the forest which supposedly "Refused to Turn to Stone." Legend says that the Warden himself had to physically "Touch" the tree to freeze it, and that his hand-print can still be seen on the trunk of the "Mother-Oak" in the center of the forest.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Still-Walkers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a group of the dispossessed who have learned how to "Navigate the Stasis." They move with a slow, rhythmic grace, having adapted their biological cycles to match the dimension’s lethargic time-flow. They wear heavy, grey robes made of stone-fiber.

The Stone-Guardians are the dominant non-humanoid lifeforms. These are massive, statuesque constructs made of the same petrified wood as the trees. They are invisible while standing still, appearing as part of the forest until they move with a grinding, tectonic speed to intercept any unauthorized traveler.

The Order of the Living Seed is a resistance faction that believes the forest is a "Planetary-Scale Seed-Bank." They spend their lives searching the petrified trunks for a specific "Fracture," hoping to find a single, living cell that they can use to "Restart Time" and return the 13th Dimension to its original, flowing state.

 Dangers & Hazards
Temporal-Petrification is the most constant threat. The "Stasis-Field" of the forest is infectious. A person who stays too long without a "Chrono-Sync" (a specialized timing device) will find their own skin beginning to harden into grey stone, eventually becoming a permanent part of the forest.

Total Silence is a psychological hazard. The lack of any auditory input can cause the brain to "Invent" its own sounds, leading to a state of permanent, light-induced madness where the traveler begins to hear the "Screams of the Stone."

Pressure-Shifts are a physical hazard. Periodically, the Chrono-Anchors will "Twitch," causing a sudden, massive spike in atmospheric pressure. This can result in "Biological-Compression," where a person is crushed into a thin sheet of leather by the weight of the air itself.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the forest is the Mother-Oak. It is rumored that deep within the hollow of the original tree, there is a single, non-petrified "Heart." Legend says that this heart is a "Time-Core," and that it contains the "Stored-Years" of every creature that was frozen during the Great Stasis.

Another rumor suggests that the Petrified Forest is not a park. Some claim it is a "Planetary-Scale Battery," and that Gabriel is using the "Stopped-Time" of the 10th Dimension to create a "Temporal-Buffer" for the Platinum Throne, allowing the Angels of the Apex to live for eons while the rest of Eldrion rots.
