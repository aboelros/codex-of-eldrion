 75. Jael's Hanging Canyons

Description: A gargantuan network of vertical, light-starved chasms carved into the dimension’s central basalt shelf, suspended over the absolute darkness of the Abyssal Breach.

---

 Overview
Jael's Hanging Canyons are the primary military and administrative hub of the 10th Dimension. While the surface of the realm is a world of stagnant time and decaying dust, the Canyons are a place of vertical intensity and high-pressure discipline. The canyons were not formed by water, but were "Etched" into the rock using concentrated beams of chronolith-energy. They reach miles into the crust, their walls so steep and smooth that they reflect the faint, flickering light of the dimension’s central power-hub.

The environment in the Canyons is one of perpetual, grey twilight and the constant, rhythmic "Thrumming" of the Warden’s thermal-pumps. The air is cold and smells of wet iron and "Static-Silt." To live in the Canyons is to live in a world of cables and ladders, where a single misstep results in a mile-long fall into the "Abyssal-Mist" below. It is the seat of Warden Jael’s power, and it is here that the dimension’s most valuable "Historical-Blueprints" are stored.

 Geography & Architecture
The Canyons are a landscape of Vertical Walls and Cable-Cities.

1. The Needle-Spires: Massive basalt pillars that rise from the canyon floors, acting as the primary anchors for the city’s hanging bridges and platforms.
2. The Shadow-Gorges: Narrow chasms where the walls are so close together that natural light never reaches the bottom, creating zones of permanent, freezing darkness.
3. The Abyssal-Rim: The lowest inhabitable levels of the canyons, where the rock meets the "Chrono-Mist" of the lower realms.

Architecture is "Vertical-Fortress" structures built into the canyon walls and suspended from the spires using miles of aetheric-iron chains. The buildings are tiered and armored, with "Lifting-Platforms" acting as the only means of transit between the levels.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Breath, the canyons were the dimension’s primary meditation-grottos. During the Carving of the Warden's Seat, Jael ordered the expansion of the fissures to create a "Defensible-Hub" that could survive the collapse of the dimension’s surface ecosystems. The excavation process supposedly took three centuries and required the labor of ten million "Still-Walkers."

History records the Fall of the Tenth Legion, a period when a group of resistance "Jumpers" attempted to sabotage the primary thermal-pump. The Warden responded by "Releasing the Chains" of an entire residential district, sending fifty thousand people into the void. Their "Echo-Screams" can still be heard in the lower gorges during the storm-cycles.

 Inhabitants & Factions
Warden Jael is the primary resident. He is a man of absolute, freezing discipline who has replaced his physical body with a harness of copper and glass. He is obsessed with "Storing the Past" and views the 10th Dimension as a massive museum that must be protected from the "Chaos of the Future" at all costs.

The Canyon-Climbers are the primary labor force. They are a specialized caste of the dispossessed who have been genetically modified with multi-jointed limbs and adhesive skin, allowing them to move across the vertical surfaces with terrifying speed. They are short-lived due to the physical stress of their work and are considered "Maintenance-Assets."

The Warden’s Elite Guard (The Stone-Falcons) are soldiers who wear gravity-resistant armor and possess the ability to "Slow-Time" in a localized area, allowing them to intercept intruders with preternatural precision.

 Dangers & Hazards
Cable-Snaps are the most constant threat. The aetheric-iron chains that hold the city are under immense stress. During a "Time-Shift" in the Wastes, the sudden change in kinetic energy can cause a chain to shatter, sending a building or a bridge into the abyss.

The Abyssal-Mist is a physical hazard. The clouds that fill the lower canyons are highly corrosive and carry "Temporal-Leeches" semi-sentient parasites that feed on the years of those they touch, causing rapid aging and death.

Vertigo-Madness is a psychological hazard. The lack of a stable ground and the infinite downward perspective can cause a state of "Gravity-Despair," where a person loses the will to hold on, eventually stepping off a platform just to end the uncertainty of the fall.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the canyons is the Abyssal Gate. It is rumored that at the absolute bottom of the deepest chasm, there is a hidden, obsidian door that leads directly to the 12th Dimension (The Abyss). Legend says that Jael is using this gate to "Deport" the most rebellious elements of his dimension, sending them into the absolute void to be erased from history.

Another rumor suggests that the Hanging Canyons are not just a city. Some claim they are a "Planetary-Scale Cooling-System," and that the "Static-Silt" of the 10th Dimension is being siphoned away through the canyons to cool the "Prime-Engine" of the Divine Apex, with the "Mist" being the resulting industrial steam.
