 62. The Eclipse Nests

Description: A collection of parasitic, lightless dwellings located on the permanently shadowed underside of the dimension's largest floating continent, the "Sol-Breaker."

---

 Overview
The Eclipse Nests are a hidden, subterranean-like society in a world that has no ground. They are located on the underside of a massive island, five hundred miles in diameter, that drifts slowly across the 8th Dimension, creating a permanent, artificial eclipse for the regions below. The Nests themselves are hanging, spherical pods made of woven obsidian-silk and "Aether-Clay," suspended from the island’s underbelly by miles of high-tensile vines.

Life in the Nests is lived entirely in the dark. The only illumination comes from the bioluminescent "Ghost-Fungi" that grows on the pod-walls and the faint, purple glow of the distant nebula. It is a world of verticality and silence, where the inhabitants move like spiders across the hanging vines. The air is cold and thin, and the sound of the world is a permanent, low-pitched whistling of the wind rushing between the dangling pods.

 Geography & Architecture
The Nests are a landscape of Hanging Pods and Inverted Verticals.

1. The Shadow-Clusters: Dense neighborhoods of hanging pods, interconnected by a web of "Vitreous-Vines" that act as the dimension’s primary streets.
2. The Hollow-Shafts: Natural vertical rifts within the island’s underbelly where the inhabitants have carved "Spite-Rooms" chambers intended to hide from the Warden’s security patrols.
3. The Resin-Plains: Areas where the pods are coated in a thick, insulating resin produced by local "Sky-Larva," creating a permanent, semi-solid surface.

Architecture is "Organic-Parasitic" structures that grow into one another, sharing life-support and energy-conduits, designed to look like natural rock formations from a distance.

 History & Lore
The Nests were established during the Age of the First Shadow, when a group of political dissidents from the 5th Dimension (The Shadow-realm) fled to the 8th to escape the Warden Noctis. They chose the underside of the Sol-Breaker because they realized that the Warden’s orbital lenses could not see through the mass of the island.

History records the Massacre of the Tethered, a period when a group of the Warden’s guards discovered the nests and attempted to "Cut the Vines." Thousands of pods fell into the void before the resistance could launch a counter-stroke. The "Falling-Memorial" is a collection of empty, lightless pods that are kept permanently drifting at the edge of the Nests in honor ofThose what were lost.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Nest-Dwellers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a group of the dispossessed who have lost their natural sight to the darkness, instead developing a "Thermo-Echo-Location" that allows them to "See" through heat and vibration. They are master "Vine-Walkers" and possess a unique "Aether-Clay" magic.

The Sky-Larva are the dominant non-humanoid lifeform. These are massive, translucent grubs that live on the underside of the islands, producing the resin used by the inhabitants. They are territorial but generally harmless if not disturbed.

The Order of the Unseen is a religious sect that believes the Eclipse is a "Holy-Veil" that protects them from the "Toxic-Light" of the Divine Apex. They spend their lives at the absolute center of the eclipse, seeking to find the "Primal-Darkness" that existed before Gabriel arrived.

 Dangers & Hazards
Vine-Snap is the most immediate threat. The Vitreous-Vines are ancient and prone to "Brittle-Fracture." During a sudden gravity-shift, a vine can snap without warning, sending a pod and its inhabitants into a terminal fall.

Sensory Blindness is a psychological hazard for outsiders. In the Nests, there is no horizon or perspective. The absolute darkness, combined with the vertical movement, can cause a state of "Void-Nausea," where a person loses their sense of up and down, eventually falling off a vine out of sheer confusion.

The Shadow-Stalkers are predators that have migrated from the Void Tear. They are invisible in the Nests and feed on the "Cohesion" of the pod-materials, causing buildings to literally melt away into nothingness.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the Nests is the Egg of the Sun. It is rumored that deep within the most secluded pod, there is a shard of "Solid-Light" that was stolen from the Platinum Throne. Legend says that this shard is being "Incubated" by the resistance, and that when it hatchs, it will create a new, "Clean-Sun" that will burn away the Warden’s control over the 8th Dimension.

Another rumor suggests that the Eclipse Nests are not just dwellings. Some claim they are a "Planetary-Scale Acoustic-Filter," and that the arrangement of the pods is intended to "Muffle the Screams of the Abyss," preventing the entities of the 12th Dimension from influencing the inhabitants of the higher realms.
