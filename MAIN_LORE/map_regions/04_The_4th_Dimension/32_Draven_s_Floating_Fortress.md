 32. Draven's Floating Fortress

Description: The mobile, obsidian-clad seat of power for Warden Draven, hovering eternally above the Colosseum of the Sun.

---

 Overview
Draven’s Floating Fortress (known locally as "The Sun-Grasp") is the primary instrument of oppression in the 4th Dimension. It is a massive, geometric structure of black obsidian and gold, kept aloft by a series of powerful "Gravity-Anchors." The fortress does not just house the Warden; it acts as a central hub for the dimension’s solar-refractor system. From its underside, a colossal magnification lens can focus the desert’s heat into a single, world-searing beam of divine light.

The interior of the fortress is a stark contrast to the blistering world below. It is kept at a constant, clinical temperature, filled with the hum of sophisticated machinery and the scent of expensive incense. It is a place of absolute order, where the only movement is the clockwork patrol of the Solar Guard and the silent service of the Warden's "Light-Thralls."

 Geography & Architecture
The Fortress is a vertical citadel composed of Floating Tiers.

1. The Observation Deck: The lowest tier, dominated by the Grand Lens and the hangar bays for the Warden’s "Sun-Chariots" (high-speed interceptors).
2. The Hall of Judgment: The middle tier, where Warden Draven receives diplomats and oversees the administrative functions of the dimension. It is decorated with trophies from the Colosseum.
3. The Zenith Spires: The uppermost towers, which house Draven's private quarters and the "Sun-Core" reactor.

The architecture is "Imperial-Solar" massive, high-ceilinged halls, floors made of polished white marble, and walls of dark, refractive glass that allow the Warden to look out over his domain without ever being seen from the outside.

 History & Lore
The Fortress was commissioned by Warden Draven shortly after he was granted his title by Gabriel. He wanted a palace that was "Between the World and the Apex," a physical representation of his authority. The construction required the labor of over a million slaves from the 5th Dimension, many of whom fell to their deaths during the assembly of the Gravity-Anchors.

History records the fortress as the site of the Protocol of the Seventh Shield, a secret meeting where Draven supposedly developed the technology to "Format the Light" of the Sun into a tactical weapon. Legend says that the fortress is not just a palace, but a "Cradle" for a new type of Angel that Draven is attempting to craft from the souls of fallen gladiators.

 Inhabitants & Factions
Warden Draven is the primary resident. He is a man obsessed with "Purity of Light," often wearing robes made of woven solar-fibers that glow with a soft, white radiance. He is brilliant, cold, and views the inhabitants of the desert as "Untempered Ore" that must be forged in the heat of his games.

The Guard of the Sun (The Solare) are his personal guard. They are elite warriors who have been "Blessed" by the Sun-Core, giving them heightened reflexes and the ability to temporarily turn their skin into living glass, making them nearly immune to physical attacks.

The Light-Thralls are the domestic servants. They are rebels who have had their "Shadows" removed by the Warden’s magics, leaving them as obedient, mindless workers who can only speak in monotone praise of the Apex.

 Dangers & Hazards
Automated Beam Defenses are the primary external hazard. The fortress is surrounded by a ring of floating "Sentry-Lenses" that automatically target anything without a valid "Solar-Key," vaporizing intruders before they can reach the hull.

Anti-Gravity Fluctuations occur near the engine rooms. A sudden shift in the Gravity-Anchors can cause a room to lose its downward pull, flinging occupants into the ceilings or walls.

The Sun-Core Leak is a lethal internal hazard. Periodically, the central reactor releases bursts of raw solar energy. Without a protective "Aetheric-Suit," exposure to these bursts will cause a person’s blood to boil instantly.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the fortress is the Sun-Core. It is whispered that the reactor is not a machine, but a Captured Piece of the Original Sun of the 13th Dimension. Legend says that Draven stole it during the Second Holy War and that he is using it to "Bio-Engineer" a new race of sun-beings that will eventually replace the human population of the desert.

Another rumor suggests that the Zenith Spires are not just rooms. Some claim they are a "Communication Relay" that speaks not to the Divine Apex, but to a Dark Entity in the Void. It is whispered that Draven is no longer loyal to Gabriel and is planning to turn the fortress’s lens toward the Apex itself when the time is right.
