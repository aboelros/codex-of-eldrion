 34. The Obsidian Quarry

Description: A massive, geometric excavation site located on the edge of the Glass Desert, where the dimension’s dense, volcanic obsidian is harvested for the Divine Apex.

---

 Overview
The Obsidian Quarry is the industrial powerhouse of the 4th Dimension. It is a series of perfectly concentric, terraced pits that descend miles into the earth, appearing from above like a black, jagged target. The quarry provides the structural glass used in the Floating Fortress, the Divine Apex’s Sea of Glass, and the high-end industrial optics of Dimension 2. 

The environment is one of overwhelming blackness and heat. The obsidian is so dense that it absorbs nearly all visible light, leaving the pits in a state of high-contrast shadows even under the eternal sun. The air is thick with "Glass-Dust" microscopic shards of black rock that shimmer in the light and shred the throat and lungs of anyone without a specialized respirator.

 Geography & Architecture
The Quarry is a vertical landscape of Sharp Terraces and Excavation Hubs.

1. The Upper Tiers: The outermost rings, where the rock is less dense and the heat is manageable. These tiers are dominated by the massive Steam-Dredges that break the rock apart.
2. The Molten Depths: The lowest levels of the quarry, where the obsidian is still semi-liquid, flowing like thick, black honey from deep volcanic vents.
3. The Sifter-Spires: Towering structures located on the quarry rim where the raw rock is sorted, polished, and magically treated before being shipped to other realms.

The architecture is purely functional and brutalist reinforced iron platforms bolted into the black glass, cranes that look like skeletal hands, and lightless "Dormitory-Cages" for the labor force.

 History & Lore
The Quarry was established during the First Reconstruction, when Gabriel decided that the architecture of the Divine Apex should be made of "Ordered Darkness" to better reflect the light of the Platinum Throne. The site was chosen because of its unique geological composition the result of a 4th Dimension moon crashing into the surface eons ago.

History records the Uprising of the Deep-Seam as the quarry’s most violent event. A group of penal workers discovered a "Void-Pocket" that caused the lower three tiers to collapse into a subterranean sea of magma. Instead of rescuing the workers, Warden Draven ordered the vents to be sealed, "Casting" the strikers directly into the obsidian they were mining.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Penal-Laborers are the primary inhabitants. These are high-risk criminals and political dissidents from across the 11 dimensions who have been sentenced to "The Hard-Dark." They are rarely expected to survive more than two years before their bodies are worn out by the heat and the glass-dust.

The Quarry-Sentinels are automated, spider-like constructs made of the very obsidian they guard. They are blind but navigate through vibrations, ensuring that the "Extraction-Quota" is met and that no worker leaves their assigned terrace.

The Veil-Miners are a secretive faction of workers who have discovered the location of the "Shadow-Veins." They are secretly harvesting small fragments of light-absorbing glass to create "Cloaking-Robes" for the resistance, acting as a vital link in the underground economy.

 Dangers & Hazards
Glass-Lacerations are a constant threat. The obsidian is sharper than surgical steel. A fall onto a terrace or a minor bump against a wall can result in wounds that are deep, clean, and notoriously difficult to heal.

Atmospheric Flash-Fire occurs in the deeper pits. The excavation process releases pockets of volatile methane and sulfur gas. A single spark from a mining tool can turn a pit into a temporary furnace of green-blue flame.

Sensory Deprivation is a psychological hazard. The light-absorbing properties of the obsidian, combined with the extreme heat, often leads to "The Black-Out," a state of hallucinations where the mind can no longer distinguish between the physical rock and the empty void.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the Quarry is the Shadow-Vein. It is rumored that at the very bottom of the central pit, there is a deposit of "Perfect Obsidian" that does not just absorb light, but Absorbs Time. Legend says that items buried in the shadow-vein are perfectly preserved, and that Warden Draven is using the vein to "Store" the physical forms of the resistance leaders until Gabriel finds a way to "Correct" their souls.

Another rumor suggests that the Obsidian Quarry is not a mine at all. Some claim it is an "Eye" a massive, planetary-scale optical lens that Gabriel is building to look into the 13th Dimension, searching for the "Original Architect" who fled the system during the First Holy War.
