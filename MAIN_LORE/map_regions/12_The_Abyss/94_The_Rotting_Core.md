 94. The Rotting_Core

Description: A gargantuan, pulsating biological power-source located at the absolute geometric center of the 12th Dimension, currently in a state of terminal, viral decay and entropic collapse.

---

 Overview
The Rotting Core is the 12th Dimension’s literal "Stomach." It is a planetary-scale biological reactor designed to process the dimension’s biological waste into arefined aetheric energy. However, due to the dimension’s extreme entropy, the reactor has become infected with a permanent, hyper-accelerated "Decay-Virus." The Core is not a machine, but a gargantuan organ a mass of pulsating, diseased muscle and "Nerve-Bundles" large enough to support entire cities.

The environment within the Core is one of clinical horror and industrial heat. The walls of the reactor "Breathe" in a slow, wet rhythm, and the air is thick with the scent of "Dormant-Decomposition" and "Saccharine-Decay." The illumination comes from the glowing "Veins" that run through the muscle-walls, carrying the blackened, toxic nutrient fluid that powers the dimension’s machines. It is a place of absolute transformation, where "Life" is merely a stage of the decay-process.

 Geography & Architecture
The Core is a landscape of Pulsating Vaults and Decay-Canals.

1. The Primary-Digestor: The central, largest chamber of the reactor, where the biological waste is physically pulverized and dissolved into the "Black-Slurry."
2. The Nervous-Gorges: Narrow, wet corridors that connect the different wards of the core. The walls here are hyper-sensitive to vibration and can detect any unauthorized movement.
3. The Filtering-Arenas: Large, circular chambers where the "Refinement-Grinders" physically separate the raw aether from the organic waste.

Architecture is "Biological-Industrial" structures that are "Grown" rather than built, with and organic-iron "Skeleton" supporting the external skin. The buildings are tiered and often feature "Exoskeletal Echoes" of the dimension’s original prehistoric leviathans.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Breath, the Core was a sacred, vital gateway known as the "Heart of Harmony." During the Disease of the Core, a period caused by Warden Arawn to "Accelerate the Recycling of the Unfit," a massive cloud of mutagenic spores was released into the reactor. The spores reacted with the dimension’s biological waste, turning the heart into the rotting, industrial engine seen today.

History records the Massacre of the Last Heartbeat, a period when a group of resistance "Bio-Scholars" attempted to "Heal the Core." The Warden responded by "Saturating" the reactor with toxic chlorine gas for a decade. The scholars did not die; they were physically "Integrated" into the reactor’s walls, becoming the "Chronic-Pain" that now vibrates through the dimension.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Core-Engineers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a highly specialized caste of the Warden’s military whose skin has been replaced with "Bio-Mechanical Apparatuses." They make their living by "Maintaining the Decay" ensuring that the reactor never stops pulsating, as its collapse would result in the physical disintegration of the entire dimension.

The Rot-Walkers (The Half-Fallen) are the dominant non-humanoid lifeforms. These are individuals who have "Drowned" in the reactor’s influence. They are visible only as green, translucent shapes that wander the nerve-corridors, attempting to "Snatch" the memories of travelers to replace the ones they have lost to the decay.

The Order of the Seed-of-the-Abyss is a resistance faction that believes the Core contains a "Universal-Truth." They spend their lives in the deepest primary-digestors, attempting to find a legendary, original "Seed" that they believe is the "Soul of the 12th Dimension." Legend says that this seed can be used to "Restart the Heart," returning the 12th Dimension to its original, healthy state.

 Dangers & Hazards
Biological-Contamination is the most constant threat. The "Chemicals" used in the core are highly mutagenic. A person who is exposed without a "Bio-Seal" suit will find their own skin beginning to "Stitch" itself into the surrounding walls, eventually becoming a permanent part of the reactor’s nervous system.

The Siphoning-Pressure is a psychological hazard. The "Heartbeat" of the reactor is powerful enough to induce a state of permanent, light-induced madness where the traveler begins to feel their own pulse "Syncing" with the core, eventually losing their own will to the collective consciousness of the machine.

Terminal-Decay is a physical hazard. certain sections of the core act as "Entropy-Sinks." Entering one of these areas is like stepping into a time-vortex; the heat and the decay can vaporize organic matter instantly.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the core is the Seed of the Abyss. It is rumored that at the absolute center of the foundation, there is a single, glowing crystal of "Primal-Soul-Aether." Legend says that this is the Physical-Blueprint for biological life, and that it is being used by the Warden to "Reprogram" the population of the lower dimensions into a state of voluntary obedience.

Another rumor suggests that the Rotting Core is not a reactor. Some claim it is a "Planetary-Scale Bio-Incinerator," and that the "Nutrient-Sludge" is actually made from the "Sins of the Astral Sea," which are physically transported to the 12th Dimension to be "Refined" out of existence before they can infect the higher realms.
