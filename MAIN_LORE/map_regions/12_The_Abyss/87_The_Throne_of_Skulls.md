 87. The Throne of Skulls

Description: A gargantuan mountain range composed entirely of trillions of fossilized humanoid and non-humanoid skulls, serving as the terrifying administrative and religious heart of the 12th Dimension.

---

 Overview
The Throne of Skulls is the physical manifestation of the 12th Dimension’s biological and spiritual "Recycling." While the Bone Mines of the 11th Dimension harvest the skeletal rock for industry, the Throne is a place of sacred and horrific "Storage." Every skull in the mountain is a record of a soul that has been "De-Synced" and "Archived" by the Divine Apex. The mountain stands twenty miles high, its surface a jagged, white-and-grey mosaic of faces from every era of Eldrion’s history.

The environment at the Throne is one of overwhelming "Psychic-Weight" and absolute, clinical silence. The air is dry and smells of old calcium, scorched iron, and the faint, sweet scent of "Decaying-Information." The light is a permanent, bruised purple twilight, and the only movements are the slow, rhythmic patrols of the Warden’s "Soul-Collectors." It is the seat of Warden Arawn’s power, and it is here that the dimension’s most valuable "Biological-Blueprints" are physically archived.

 Geography & Architecture
The Throne is a landscape of Skeletal Slopes and Bone-Plateaus.

1. The Ossuary-Peaks: The highest summits of the mountain, where the skulls of the dimension’s original rulers are stored in intricate, geometric patterns.
2. The River of Eye-Sockets: A narrow, vertical gorge where the "Sorrow-Silt" from the higher realms cascades down the mountain-face in a slow-moving, grey waterfall.
3. The Foundation-Ribs: Massive, miles-long arcs of compressed bone that support the weight of the mountain, appearing like the ribs of a gargantuan buried beast.

Architecture is "Gothic-Skeletal" structures carved directly into the bone-slopes and reinforced with iron-straps. The Warden’s primary palace The Crown of Skulls is a needle-sharp spire of "Obsidian-and-Bone" that rises from the highest peak, visible from every corner of the dimension.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Breath, the mountain was a sacred place of memory and celebration. During the Forging of the Throne, Warden Arawn decided that "Individual-Identity" was a source of rebellion. He ordered the systematic "Archiving" of every skull in the dimension to create a "Monument to the warden’s Will," turning the realm into the planetary-scale mortuarium it is today.

History records the Massacre of the Silent-Scream, a period when a group of resistance "Necro-Scholars" attempted to "Wake the Mountain" using high-level acoustic weapons. The Warden responded by "Inverting the Psychic-Filter," causing the scholars to physically "Merge" with the mountain-face. Their faces are still visible on the slopes of the central peak, frozen in a state of permanent, petrified horror.

 Inhabitants & Factions
Warden Arawn (The Shadow-King) is the primary resident. He is a man of absolute, freezing discipline who has replaced his physical body with a harness of copper and dark-glass. He is obsessed with "Maintaining the Archive" and views the 12th Dimension as a massive library that must be protected from the "Chaos of the Future" at all costs.

The Soul-Collectors (The Bone-Wraiths) are the dimension’s primary security. These are semi-solid entities made of drifting ash and "Broken-Memory." They hunt the skeletal slopes and are programmed to "Extract" the information from any unauthorized traveler, adding their skulls to the mountain-face.

The Order of the First-Skull is a resistance faction that believes the mountain contains an "Original-Identity." They spend their lives searching the deepest foundations for the legendary, "Sacred-Skull" of the first thinking being ever produced by Gabriel. Legend says that this skull contains the "Access-Keys" to the Divine Apex’s central servers.

 Dangers & Hazards
Psychic-Assimilation is the most constant threat. The "Psychic-Weight" of trillions of archived souls can cause the brain to shut down, leading to a state of "Ego-Death" where the traveler begins to "See" through the eyes of the skulls on the mountain, eventually becoming part of the mountain-face.

Bone-Dust-Lung is a physical hazard. The slopes are covered in a permanent layer of fine calcium-dust. A person who stays too long without a "Seal-Mask" will find their own lungs beginning to harden into stone, eventually becoming a "Living-Statue."

The Shifting-Slopes are a topological hazard. The mountain is fundamentally unstable. A single seismic shift in the dimension’s crust can cause a "Skeletal-Avalanche" that can bury an entire district under millions of tons of stone.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the throne is the Original Skull. It is rumored that at the absolute center of the foundation, there is a single, glowing skull made of "Primal-Soul-Aether." Legend says that this is the Physical-Blueprint for the Human Soul, and that it is being used by the Warden to "Reprogram" the population of the lower dimensions into a state of voluntary obedience.

Another rumor suggests that the Throne of Skulls is not a mountain. Some claim it is a "Planetary-Scale Hard-Drive," and that Gabriel is using the "Entropy" of the 12th Dimension to process the "Discarded-Data" of the entire Eldrion population, with each skull representing a different, deleted civilization.
