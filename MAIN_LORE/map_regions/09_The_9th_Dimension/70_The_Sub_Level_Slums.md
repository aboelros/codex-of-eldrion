 70. The Sub-Level Slums

Description: A light-starved, subterranean urban sprawl located at the geometric base of the Prismatic Spire, housing the dimension’s "Refracted" the outcasts and laborers deemed too messy for the surface.

---

 Overview
The Sub-Level Slums are the dark secret of the 9th Dimension’s perfection. While the surface is a world of absolute light and mathematical precision, the Slums are a world of grey shadows and industrial grime. Built within the gargantuan "Foundation-Chambers" of the Prismatic Spire, the slums are a labyrinth of cramped tunnels, salvaged glass huts, and narrow alleys that are never touched by the direct light of the Apex.

The atmosphere in the slums is heavy, damp, and smells of "Ozone-Silt" and stagnant aetheric-runoff. The only illumination is the "Flicker-Light" the weak, dying luminescence of recycled light-tubes and the distant, silver glow of the Spire’s primary stabilizers. It is a place of desperate survival, where "Lux-Credits" (vials of stored light) are the only currency and where the "Rainbow-Plague" is a constant, terrifying reality.

 Geography & Architecture
The Slums are a landscape of Foundation-Fissures and Silt-Sinks.

1. The Spire-Roots: Massive, miles-long crystalline pillars that support the tower. The slum-dwellers have carved their homes directly into the sides of these pillars, creating the "Inverted-Suburbs."
2. The Runoff-Canals: Narrow channels where the chemical waste from the Light Weaver’s Guild collects. The water here is a glowing, toxic purple and provides the only heat for the district.
3. The Shadow-Pockets: Dead-zones in the crystalline geometry where light literally cannot reach, creating patches of absolute, terrifying darkness.

Architecture is "Salvaged-Crystalline" structures built from broken shards of Luce-Quartz and reinforced with rusted iron from Dimension 2. Buildings lean against one another for support, forming a three-dimensional web of improvised habitation.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Prism, the base of the spire was a clean, sacred foundation. During the Great Casting-Down, which followed the Massacre of the Spectrum-Breakers, the Warden decreed that all "Imperfect-Citizens" were to be relocated to the sub-levels to serve as the maintenance-slaves for the Spire’s stabilizers.

History records the Uprising of the Dim-Light, a period when the slum-dwellers attempted to "Short-Circuit" the Spire by dumping chemical waste into the primary light-wells. The Warden, Lux, responded by "Flushing" the upper slum districts with super-heated steam, incinerating ten thousand people in an hour and leaving the "Steam-Scars" that still mark the foundation pillars today.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Refracted are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are individuals who have been "Digitally-Branded" as imperfect. They possess a unique "Low-Light Sight" and are experts in scavenging the Spire’s industrial waste. They are bitter, resourceful, and deeply suspicious of any "Surface-Sun."

The Sound-Dogs are the dominant wildlife. These are biological constructs eyeless, canine-like entities that use acoustic-sonar to hunt in the dark. They were originally designed as security drones but have since gone rogue, haunting the runoff-canals.

The Order of the True Shadow is a resistance faction that believes the darkness of the slums is a "Holy-Protection." They seek to find the "Under-Core," a legendary, original piece of the Spire’s foundation that they believe can be used to "Turn off the Sun" and plunge the entire 9th Dimension into a state of peaceful, pre-Holy War darkness.

 Dangers & Hazards
The Rainbow-Plague is the primary medical threat. It is a disease caused by exposure to the Spire’s toxic runoff, causing the skin to break out in multi-colored, glowing pustules that eventually "Explode" into raw, uncontrollable light, killing the host.

Structural-Cave-ins are a physical hazard. The foundation-fissures are under immense pressure from the Spire’s weight. Minor seismic shifts in the dimension can cause massive blocks of Luce-Quartz to shatter, burying entire districts under millions of tons of glass.

Lux-Withdrawal is a psychological hazard. The human body requires a minimum amount of light to function. In the deeper slums, people often suffer from "Gray-Madness" a state of total lethality and despair caused by the lack of natural solar radiation.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the slums is the Under-Core. It is rumored that deep beneath the lowest level of the Spire-Roots, there is a hidden, unstable reactor that the resistance has built using stolen components. Legend says that this core is the "Counter-Sun," and that it is being used to power a secret "Gateway to the 5th Dimension" (Shadow-realm).

Another rumor suggests that the Sub-Level Slums are not a prison. Some claim they are a "Biological-Stabilizer," and that the "Suffering" of the Refracted is being physically processed by the Spire’s crystals to "Balance" the perfection of the surface light, preventing the 9th Dimension from spontaneously shattering under its own mathematical purity.
