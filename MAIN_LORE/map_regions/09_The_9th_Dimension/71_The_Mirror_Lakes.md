 71. The Mirror Lakes

Description: Expansive, perfectly still basins of liquid silver-aether that reflect the dimension’s light and the subconscious projections of those who gaze into them.

---

 Overview
The Mirror Lakes are the most beautiful and spiritually dangerous region of the 9th Dimension. Located at the "Low-Horizon" of the central continent, the lakes are not made of water, but of "Liquid-Luce" a metallic, mercury-like suspension of condensed light and silver. The surface of these lakes is so still and so perfectly reflective that it is often impossible to tell where the sky ends and the ground begins, creating a world of total, breathtaking symmetry.

The environment of the Mirror Lakes is one of absolute silence and "Reflective Melancholy." The air is cold and smells of ozone and polished metal. The lakes have the unique property of reflecting not just the physical world, but the "Aetheric-Record" of whoever is standing on the shore. A person might see their own past, their deepest fears, or versions of themselves that never were, perfectly rendered in the liquid silver. It is a place of pilgrimage for the broken and a source of terror for those with secrets.

 Geography & Architecture
The Lakes are a landscape of Silver Basins and Memory-Isles.

1. The Basin of Eternity: The largest of the lakes, a fifty-mile-wide circle of liquid silver that reflects the entire 9th Dimension sky.
2. The Memory-Islands: Small outcrops of obsidian that float within the liquid, upon which the "Memory-Merchants" have built their pavilions.
3. The Shores of Glass: Beaches made of perfectly white silica sand that has been "Glazed" by the intense solar light, appearing like white porcelain.

Architecture is "Ethereal-Classical" pavilions made of white marble and "Light-Glass," with open courtyards designed to maximize the view of the lakes. The buildings are often built on "Floating-Foundations" that allow them to drift slowly across the silver surface.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Prism, the lakes were the dimension’s primary "Oraclepools," used by the high-priests to consult the Astral Sea. During the Era of Ordering, Warden Lux "Sealed" the lakes with silver-aether, turning the open water into the reflective mirror seen today, claiming that "Truth is found in the reflection of the Warden's Will."

History records the Massacre of the False-Self, a period when a group of rebels attempted to "Drown the Warden" in his own reflection. The Warden purportedly "Swapped Places" with his reflection and watched from within the lake as the rebels were vaporized by his own holographic body. Legend says that the "Original-Warden" is still trapped beneath the surface of the Basin of Eternity, waiting for someone to "Break the Mirror."

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Memory-Merchants are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a specialized caste of scholars who have learned how to "Extract" images from the lakes and store them in crystalline "Memory-Vials." They sell these vials as a form of entertainment or psychological therapy to the elite of the higher dimensions.

The Purity-Drones are the dimension’s primary security. These are small, spherical constructs made of polished mirror-glass. They float above the lakes, constantly scanning the reflections for any "Mathematical-Inconsistency" or "Spiritual-Discord," which they correct with bursts of high-intensity light.

The Order of the True Reflection is a resistance faction that believes the lakes are a "Locked-Library." They spend their lives studying the subtle distortions in the silver, hoping to find the "Original-Record" of Eldrion a reflection of the world as it was before Gabriel and the Divine Apex rewritten history.

 Dangers & Hazards
Silver-Poisoning (Liquid-Light Toxicity) is a constant threat. The "Liquid-Luce" is highly toxic to organic life. A single drop on the skin can cause "Refraction-Canker" a condition where the skin turns into non-functioning glass.

Psychological Fragmentation is the primary psychological hazard. Gazing into the lakes for too long can cause a person to "Lose themselves in the Reflections." They might begin to believe that their reflection is the real person, eventually wandering into the lake to "Join" their past self and drowning in the silver fluid.

Mirror-Storms occur during high-sun events. The surface of the lakes becomes volatile, and the liquid silver can be kicked up into the air, creating a blizzard of million tiny, razor-sharp mirrors that can shred a traveler in seconds.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the lakes is the True Reflection. It is rumored that there is a single, small lake hidden in a deep obsidian gorge that does not reflect the current 9th Dimension. Legend says that this lake shows "Alaria" the original world of the 13th Dimension and that anyone who dives into it will be physically transported back to the golden age before the Holy Wars.

Another rumor suggests that the Mirror Lakes are not natural. Some claim they are a "Planetary-Scale Buffer," and that the Warden is using the silver-aether to "Absorb the Collective Guilt" of the 9th Dimension’s population, preventing their combined "Sorrow" from disturbing the perfection of the Prismatic Spire.
