 21. The Scrap Peaks

Description: To the east of the Slums lie the Scrap Peaks mountains made entirely of compressed, rusting industrial waste and discarded prototypes.

---

 Overview
The Scrap Peaks are a monument to the 2nd Dimension's "Discard Culture." For centuries, the waste-chutes of the Piston City and the Great Bellows have dumped millions of tons of failed projects, broken gears, and contaminated metals into the eastern plains. Under the weight of their own mass and the periodic heating from the Slag Wastes, these piles have compressed into a range of artificial mountains that rival the natural geography of any other dimension.

The Peaks are a jagged, orange landscape where every surface is a potential blade. The air is thin and tastes of iron, and the "Weather" consists of frequent "Metal-Storms" where the wind picks up millions of sharp iron filings. It is a place of absolute lawlessness, serving as a sanctuary for those who have been "Expelled from the System."

 Geography & Architecture
The Peaks are not solid rock, but a "Loose Integration" of industrial trash.

1. The Razor-Ridges: The highest summits, where the metal has been sharpened by the wind into edges that can slice through leather easily.
2. The Hollow-Valleys: Depressions between the peaks where larger pieces of machinery crashed airships, massive piston-heads, and hullplates have created a natural, if unstable, network of caves and tunnels.
3. The Magnet-Basins: Low areas where the concentration of iron is so high that they create localized magnetic fields. These basins pull in any nearby metal, including gear and weapons from unwary travelers.

There is no formal architecture, but the inhabitants have built Scrap-Forts improvised defensive structures made of welded plates and reinforced with the ribs of ancient machines.

 History & Lore
In the Age of Discovery, the Scrap Peaks were merely a series of waste-heaps. However, during the Great Refinement, the Warden realized that the "Discarded Prototypes" in the heaps were potentially dangerous if they fell into the wrong hands. He ordered the peaks to be "Compacted" using orbital gravity-beams, resulting in the mountainous structures seen today.

Legend says that the Peaks contain the "Ancestors of the Gears" the original prototypes for the machines of the Divine Apex. Somewhere in the deep heart of the mountains, the first "Clockwork Angel" is supposedly buried, its circuits still humming with the original, un-corrupted vision of Gabriel.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Outcast Clans are the primary inhabitants. These are the descendants of those who were exiled for "Industrial Subversion" or "Genetic Impurity." They are skilled mechanics who have survived by "Salvage-Living," building everything from their homes to their prosthetic limbs from the surrounding scrap.

The Scrap-Vultures are the Peaks' most common wildlife. They are large, flightless mechanical birds constructed from salvaged gears and leather. They "Eat" the rust from the peaks, and they are notoriously territorial, attacking anyone who attempts to mine the "Pure-Iron" veins of the higher summits.

There is a rumor of a faction known as the Mechanics of the Void. These are former engineers from the Piston City who have gone mad and now believe that the Scrap Peaks are a "Living God" that must be rebuilt into a new, mechanical deity.

 Dangers & Hazards
Metal-Avalanches are the most common threat. The peaks are structurally unstable. A loud noise or a sudden shift in wind can cause thousands of tons of rusted iron to slide down the slopes, burying everything in their path.

Lightning-Conduction is a lethal weather hazard. Because the peaks are made of metal, they act as a massive lightning rod. During the "Atmospheric-Discharges" common in Dimension 2, the peaks become electrified, turning every surface into a high-voltage deathtrap.

Tetanus-Mist is a biological threat. The orange "Rust-Dust" in the valleys is so concentrated that it can penetrate the skin. Once in the bloodstream, it causes "The Grinding," a condition where the person’s joints begin to lock up and their skin turns the color of oxidized copper.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the Peaks is the Archive of the Pre-Apex Kings. It is whispered that deep within the "Foundational Tiers" of the mountains, the original library of the 2nd Dimension is preserved. This library allegedly contains the blueprints for the "Anti-Gravity Engines" that allowed the ancient civilizations to escape the pull of the Divine Apex.

Another rumor suggests that the Scrap Peaks are not just waste. Some claim that the Warden is intentionally building the mountains into a specific shape a "Planetary Engine" that, when completed, will allow him to pilot the entire 2nd Dimension out of its orbit and away from the control of Gabriel's throne.
