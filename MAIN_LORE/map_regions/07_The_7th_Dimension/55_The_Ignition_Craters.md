 55. The Ignition Craters

Description: A geological and industrial complex of massive geothermic pits located at the "Core-Rift" of the 7th Dimension, where the realm’s internal magma is harvested for the Divine Apex.

---

 Overview
The Ignition Craters are the primary power generators of the 7th Dimension. They are not natural volcanoes, but "Excavated Vents" miles-wide circular pits that have been drilled through the basalt crust to reach the semi-liquid mantle of the dimension. The craters act as massive thermal furnaces, where the heat of the core is funneled through "Aetheric-Turbines" to generate the energy required for the higher dimensions’ heavy industry.

The environment within the craters is absolute, incandescent chaos. The air is a thick, orange haze of sulfur and vaporized rock, and the sound is a permanent, tectonic roar of shifting magma and roaring steam. The heat is so intense that even the air itself can ignite if a "Methane-Pocket" is breached. To work in the craters is to live in a world of blinding light and crushing pressure, where the boundary between the ground and the fire is constantly shifting.

 Geography & Architecture
The Craters are a landscape of Molten Basins and Pressure-Vents.

1. The Magma-Seas: The central flooring of the larger craters, composed of deep pools of liquid rock that flow with a slow, grinding inevitability.
2. The Steam-Needles: Narrow, vertical obsidian pillars that rise from the magma, acting as vents for the high-pressure gases generated by the core.
3. The Siphoning-Platforms: Massive, cantilevered iron structures built into the crater walls, which house the turbines and the "Heat-Siphons."

Architecture is "Gothic-Industrial-Heavy" reinforced iron bolted into the rock with mile-long titanium screws, shielded with "Lead-Glass" to protect the machinery from the thermal radiation.

 History & Lore
In the Age of the First Flame, the craters were simple hot springs. During the Tapping of the Core, Gabriel ordered the dimension’s engineers to "Open the Earth’s Heart." The drilling process caused a series of "Dimensional-Earthquakes" that shattered the original tectonic plates, creating the deep, boiling rifts that exist today.

History records the Massacre of the Fourth Vent, a period when a group of penal workers sabotaged a pressure-regulating valve. The resulting explosion vaporized the entire crater and sent a "Magma-Plume" into the high atmosphere, permanently altering the dimension’s weather patterns and creating the "Rain of Cinders" that periodically falls on the Slums.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Magma-Divers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. These are specialized workers who wear "Phase-Shifters" suits that temporarily turn their physical bodies into heat-resistant light. They dive into the magma-seas to repair the deep-conduits, and they are the highest-paid (and shortest-lived) workers in the dimension.

The Warden’s Fire-Constructs are massive, multi-limbed robots made of fused obsidian and iron. They are powered directly by the magma they guard and are programmed to "Stabilize" any thermal anomaly which usually involves incinerating any unauthorized intruder.

The Order of the Sun-Grasp is a faction of the resistance that believes the heat of the Craters is a "Captured Star." They seek to "Overload" the turbines, intending to create a thermal explosion large enough to shatter the dimension’s crust and send the 7th Dimension back into the Astral Sea.

 Dangers & Hazards
Magma-Splashes are the most constant threat. The pools are volatile and prone to sudden, explosive venting. A single drop of liquid rock can melt through standard-issue armor and flesh in a millisecond.

Sudden Pressure-Venting is a physical hazard. The "Steam-Needles" can release high-pressure gas without warning. The force of the venting is enough to "Sunder" a settlement and send its inhabitants into the magma below.

Thermal-Deafening is a sensory hazard. The roar of the core-rift is so intense that it can cause permanent neurological damage and even cause the internal organs to vibrate apart if a person enters a "Sound-Focus" zone without protection.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the craters is the Heart of the Sun. It is rumored that at the bottom of the deepest crater, there is a single, glowing crystal of "Primal-Aether." Legend says that this is the Physical Soul of the 7th Dimension, and that it is being slowly "Drained" by Gabriel to power the Platinum Throne of Realm 0.

Another rumor suggests that the Ignition Craters are not just furnaces. Some claim they are a "Biological-Refinery," and that the "Raw-Material" for the 9th Dimension’s flesh-constructs is being "Cauterized" and "Purified" in the heat of the 7th Dimension before it is sent to the lower realms.
