 54. The Pyrian Cinder Slums

Description: A sprawling, chaotic urban sprawl of scrap metal and petrified ash, located on the precarious edge of the Ignition Craters.

---

 Overview
The Pyrian Cinder Slums are the industrial underbelly of the 7th Dimension. Built by the dimension’s "Unrefined" labor force, the slums are a testament to survival in an environment that was never meant for human life. The buildings are constructed from scavenged shards of obsidian, corrugated iron from Dimension 2, and a unique, hardened paste made from mixing volcanic ash with the red runoff of the 5th Dimension.

The atmosphere in the slums is one of permanent, oily smoke and the constant, rhythmic "thump" of the nearby Ignition Craters’ thermal-pumps. The heat here is not as absolute as on the Plateaus, but it is a "Wet-Heat" a humid, suffocating environment where sweat never evaporates and the air tastes of sulfur and burnt iron. It is a place of desperate trade, where water is the only currency that matters and where life is as cheap as a handful of cinder-sand.

 Geography & Architecture
The Slums are a landscape of Vertical Alleys and Scrap-Towers.

1. The Ash-Basement: The lowest levels of the slums, built into the deep fissures of the crater-rim where the temperature is slightly cooler but the toxic gases are most concentrated.
2. The Steam-Alleys: Narrow, winding corridors where the waste-heat pipes from the Ignition Craters are located. These alleys are prized for their "Free-Energy," but they are also death-traps during a pressure-venting cycle.
3. The Cinder-Peaks: Makeshift buildings that reach several stories high, built on the very edge of the craters to maximize the surface area for solar-collection.

The architecture is "Industrial-Vernacular" improvised, leaning structures that use the "Bellows-Effect" of the wind to provide natural cooling. Every building is covered in a thick layer of oily soot that provides a primitive form of insulation against the solar glare.

 History & Lore
The Slums were established during the Age of Reconstruction, when a massive influx of laborers from the 1st and 3rd Dimensions was brought in to build the dimension’s energy-infrastructure. Gabriel’s engineers provided no housing, so the workers built their own settlements using the industrial waste that was dumped into the craters.

History records the Uprising of the Steam-Fist, a period when the slum-dwellers attempted to "Choke the Apex" by sabotaging the thermal-pumps. The Warden, Pyre, responded by "Flushing" the steam-alleys with super-heated magma, incinerating three entire districts in an hour. The survivors built the Memorial of the Scorched, a jagged spire of melted scrap that stands at the center of the slums today.

 Inhabitants & Factions
The Cinder-Scavengers are the primary humanoid inhabitants. They are a tough, resourceful people who make their living by "Mining the Scraps" finding valuable minerals and machinery fragments in the industrial runoff of the craters. They wear "Soot-Robes" and heavy, brass-framed goggles.

The Fire-Brands is the primary resistance faction. They are headed by a former engineer known as Kaelen "The Smelter," who believes that the dimension’s heat can be used as a weapon against the Warden. They operate secret forges in the deep fissures, crafting "Heat-Absorbing" weapons and armor for the coming war.

The Warden’s Overseers are its primary oppressors. These are lower-level angels who have been "Disgraced" and sent to manage the labor force. They are bitter, cruel, and prone to using their "Light-Whips" to enforce quotas at the slightest provocation.

 Dangers & Hazards
Thermal-Flashfires are a daily occurrence. The concentration of oily dust and methane gas in the narrow alleys makes the slums a tinderbox. A single spark from a forge or a minor solar flare can turn a city block into a furnace in seconds.

Ash-Lung is a chronic and eventually fatal condition caused by inhaling the soot of the slums. It causes the lungs to slowly fill with a hardened, carbon-like substance, leading to a permanent, wracking cough and eventually total respiratory failure.

Crater-Collapses are a physical hazard. The land on which the slums are built is structurally unstable volcanic rock. Large sections of the slums have been known to "Slough-Off" into the Ignition Craters during seismic events, taking thousands of inhabitants with them into the magma.

 Secrets & Rumors
The greatest secret of the slums is the Deep-Forge. It is rumored that beneath the Ash-Basement, the resistance has built a massive, secret industrial complex that is powered by a "Stolen Spark" from the Divine Apex. Legend says that they are building a "Mechanical Titan" made of fused obsidian that will eventually rise from the craters to topple the Warden’s palace.

Another rumor suggests that the Cinder Slums are not just a labor camp. Some claim it is a "Planetary-Scale Bio-Incinerator," and that the "Ash" produced by the dimension is not from rock, but from the "Sins of the Astral Sea," which are physically transported to the 7th Dimension to be burned out of existence before they can infect the higher realms.
